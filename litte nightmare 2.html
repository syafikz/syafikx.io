The School

 

Sneak through the broken door on the right and over into the next room. Here, you’ll find a bunch of TVs stacked on top of each other. These will come in handy later. For now, Six will boost you through the window. Now, you have to get her over.

There will be a hook swinging in the middle of the room. Jump from the TV on the right over to it and swing into the TV on the left. Keep kicking it until it knocks over, and this will shoot you upwards. Then, swing over to the left and jump onto the platform.

Head through the door on the left and push the remaining TV down. This will lift Six up to the level above you. Now, head back out the doorway on the right and meet her by the stairs. You’ll have to jump and grab her hand, so make sure to get a running start.

Once you’re over, continue heading up the stairs. Through the doorway on the left, you’ll find glitching remains. After doing that, head back to the right and cross over the planks. Head through the door on your right, into the hallway, and through the crack in the next door.

Here, a TV will turn on and instantly get Mono's attention. Head towards it and tune the transmission to head to your next location.

 

Once you’re back on your feet, head right through the open window. Continue moving through the alley until you reach a dumpster. With Six’s help, move it over to reveal a hole in the fence, then crawl through.

This will lead you to the School. Before heading in, though, you can find glitching remains in the back by a tricycle and a hat on the dumpster by the front door.

Then, climb up the rope on the side of the building, then through the window. You’ll notice a switch on the wall, flip it and it will turn off the lights. You’ll see a tiny bit of light shining through one of the pictures on the wall. Now, you’ll have to find a way to knock it down.

Head down the hallway and enter the room on the left that’s filled with bunk beds. Grab an object off the floor, and head back to the picture. Toss this item at it and it’ll fall down, then crawl through the hole.

You’ll then find yourself in a room with a chair in the middle. If you move around the floor a bit, you’ll notice some unstable flooring. With Six’s help, jump up and down on it until it breaks.

From here, continue making your way under the floor until you reach a drop off. Jump down on the platforms until you’re at the bottom, then remove the vent on the right. Crawl through until you drop on the other side near some lockers.

Here, you’ll see the shadow of the Teacher. After she closes the door and walks away, jump over the locker and continue moving forward.

Go through the doorway with light shining out of it. Pay attention to the floor, though, as there are triggers that will send a bucket swinging your way if you step on them. So, jump over it, then move under the light and keep following the light through the door.

Tip: If Six gets scared and won’t move, go back and hold her hand while walking with her.

Another trigger is on the floor here, so watch out. The room to your right is also a trap, so don't go through there. You’ll notice a Bully at the end of the next hallway as well. Navigate this one carefully, as there's another trigger on the floor that will crush you with a locker if you hit it. However, if you do step on it, then quickly run backwards to avoid being hit.

Once it’s landed, climb on top of the locker that fell, then onto the ones on your right to head through that door.

While moving through this room, you’ll see a toy duck on the floor. Make sure to duck down while going near it, as a light will come swinging down towards you. Once it’s settled, climb out from under it, jump on top of it, and keep moving forward into the next room.

Keep moving down the hallway, but Bullies will trap you under a locker and take Six away! Once you’re out from under there, head left. You’ll see them take her up a ladder, so now you have to find a way up there.

Head right down the hallway again and jump over the locker that fell on you. Pick up the hammer next to it and move towards the Bully by the door. Smash him with it, then go through the door.

You’ll encounter another bully in this room, but stick close to the wall here, as one of their own buckets will swing down and kill him. Then, climb up on the chair by the door and through the hole.

Open the third locker here to find glitching remains. Then, you’ll find an elevator that needs a key, so continue moving forward down the hall.

This is the Teacher’s classroom, so you’ll have to move quietly. Crouch down and move from desk to desk while she’s facing the chalkboard.

Once you’ve gotten into the room with the filing cabinets, you’ll notice the key on top of a book shelf. Start climbing it, but be prepared to jump off, as it’s going to come falling down. Run and hide in the open box before the Teacher gets there.

She’ll stretch out her neck and check out the room, but then she’ll leave and shut the door. This will knock off the vent nearby, so grab the key and sneak through there. You’ll pop out on the other side through a cupboard, and now you’ll have to sneak around her as she moves around.

The best way to avoid her is to go behind her desk. Once she turns away from the entrance on the left, make a break for it. Her neck can only stretch so far, so run all the way to the elevator, use the key, and head up.

Once you’re out of the elevator, head through the door on the right. Here, you’ll find a Bully wearing what looks like one of the Nome’s hats and scribbling with chalk. There’ll be a pipe right next to him.

However, he’ll notice your presence and immediately start coming after you. Thankfully, he’s tied up to the wall. So bring him out far enough to where he yanks back. Then, when he’s down, run and grab the pipe and smack him with it.

Once he’s down, take the pipe over the door and smash a bigger hole in it to go through. Once you’re through, climb up the dresser and through the vent in the wall. This will lead you into an area above the room with the file cabinets.

Unfortunately, the Teacher’s down there and, even worse, she heard you. Start running across the plank next to you and hide in the box. Wait until her head goes back down all the way, then slowly make your way across to the other side.

You’ll have to move a jar out of the way in order to fully cross the plank, so be prepared to run and hide in the next box ahead of you. Wait until her neck goes all the way back down, then start climbing up the knotted rope.

Follow the plank to the left over to the next area, then follow the next plank. You’re going to have to knock this next plank down to reach the vent, so do that quickly, then run before her head catches up to you.

Follow the vents until you wind up in a library. She’ll end up walking in there too. Drop down from the top shelf and make your way over to the ladder. First, push it to the second book shelf to find a collectible hat. Then, push it all the way to the right and climb up onto the shelf and down onto the one in front of it.

This will send a book flying to the floor, which will catch her attention. Get ready to run and slide under the shelf ahead of you. Then, jump onto the stack of books held together by ropes. You’ll have to jump between these, but the second one will send some books toppling over. She’ll stretch her head to find you, starting on the left side then right, but you can avoid her by moving around.

After jumping over the banister, she’ll head through the door. This is also your way out. Grab the box of books by the bookshelf and move it near the door. Then, jump up and twist the handle.

Once you’re through, start making your way through the hallway until you hit a staircase. The door to the right of this staircase requires a key, so head upstairs. There’s glitching remains to your left, and a door to open on your right.

Put the top on the white rook to make a platform to stand on. Then, jump to the handle to open the door. This will lead you into a room with a strange, broken chessboard on the ground, including one of the Bullies as a piece.

Continue heading right and you’ll see a picture with a red eye drawn on it. Jump up and pull the bottom to reveal the key to solving this puzzle. You need to find the tops to the missing pieces. Head to the right to find the queen, the king is on the table to the left of the board, and the rook is on the piece outside of this room.

In order to reach the king, just place the rook piece in the queen’s spot temporarily. That way, you can climb on top of it to reach the table. Once everything’s on, a light will flicker on your right. Jump over to it via the top of the rook and pull down on the light. This will open a secret door where you’ll find the key.

Now, grab the key and head back downstairs to unlock the door. This will open up into the kitchen area. Grab the cart on your left and move it over to the vent on your right. However, before climbing in the vent, go through the door on the left. You'll find glitching remains here. Then, head back out and climb up to the vent.

You’ll land in the kitchen. After jumping over the counter, you’ll notice several Bullies doing various things around you. So, when you jump down, quickly grab the ladle and get ready to fight off four of them. They’ll come at you quickly, so make sure to swing early since it takes Mono longer to lift items.

There are several more on the other side, but you have to be careful of how you approach this next area. Thankfully, when you hit one of the Bullies before, it knocked off their head. So, look around the floor to find it and put it on. Then, sneak through the vent under the table on the far right and past the Bullies here.

When you move underneath the table, one of the Bullies will push over a bowl. If it hits you, it’ll break your disguise and you’ll die. So, let the Bully push it until it crashes onto the floor, then continue forward. Some Bullies will also push you if you walk into them, so be wary of where you’re moving.

Keep moving through the hallway until you reach the door at the end, then sneak through the crack until you’re safe on the other side. In this next room, climb the shelves in the back right corner and jump onto the shelves on the left.

Follow the top of this shelf over to the next shelf until you hit a plank. Cross this and jump to the shelves across from you to continue climbing up. Then, jump to the noose, and swing to the other side.

At the end of this path, you’ll notice a hook and a brain in a jar. Throw the brain in the jar down so it smashes, then take the hook back down to the bottom. Then, pick up the brain and throw it at the button on the wall to go into the next room.

The Teacher is back again, so get ready to sneak around her. Go through the vent on the wall and crawl under the table. Stay there until she moves, then move to the next spot over, then the next spot over after she goes in the other room.

Then, follow her through the door and hide under the table on the left. Unfortunately, you’re going to have to climb on the counter tops now. So move to the right and climb up the drawers and duck behind the jars. When she turns around, slowly make your way across using jars and books as cover.

Then, at the end of this area, jump over to the other side and jump towards the opening in the wall. Once you’re beyond the door, climb up the bookcase and into the vent.

Once you’re out of the vent, you’ll drop into an area with more Bullies. Thankfully, there’s a hammer on the ground to use, but don’t pick it up yet. The second plank on the floor will trigger a bucket to fall, so step on it quickly then run back and grab the hammer.

The best way to fight these guys is to stay where you are and let them run to you. Otherwise, moving around the bucket with the hammer can get a bit too complicated. Once the three in this room are handled, make your way into the hallway with your hammer.

More will come after you, so be ready to hit them. There’s also a bucket at the end of the hallway that’s triggered by a plank on the floor. So, when you reach the door, stick to the side to avoid being hit. Then, make your way through the door.

Make sure to keep your hammer with you, as this is where you’ll finally rescue Six. After taking down the remaining Bullies, hit the plank holding her up with your hammer and she’ll drop to the ground. Move towards her to interact with her, and once she’s back on her feet, continue moving forward.

Jump on the cabinet and lift up the window to head outside. Cross the plank over into the next building and you’ll enter into a room with a piano in it.

There’s a crank to the back right of it, so head back there and start turning it. This will lift the piano up a little ways, then it’ll drop and break the flooring. However, it’s not all the way, so you and Six will have to jump on it until it fully breaks through the floor.

In this next room, there’s a vent on the left side that Six will boost you through. On the other side is a Bully that’s holding a key. While the Bully is busy playing with it, drop down quietly and sneak over to the piece of wood, which you can use as cover.

Six will then distract the Bully by stepping on the piano, giving you some time to grab the pipe that was next to the Bully and smack it. Then, head over to the door on your right and Six will help you open it a crack to go back into the room.

Then, head over to the door and unlock it. This will lead you into a room with another Bully nearby. There’s a hammer nearby, but Six will beat you to it by taking this one down herself. Then, pull out the drawer in the dresser to your right to hop over.

Take the stairs up to the next floor. There’s a closed door at the end of the hallway, but Six will boost you up onto the shelves. At the top, there’s a box you’ll need to push over in order for Six to climb up with you.

Once she’s up there, lift open the vent and crawl through. You’re now back in a room with the Teacher, who’s playing piano. Climb down the books until you’re at the floor, then quietly make your way across to the crank on the right. Only move it when she’s playing, but this will lower the platform down so you can cross to the next vent.

Then, move the stool over to the shelving you came down from earlier so you can get back up. Again, only move while she’s playing, otherwise she’ll hear you.

Make your way back up and cross the platform over to the next vent. Unfortunately, she heard you open it, so get ready to run. She’ll extend her neck through the vents to follow you, so move quickly.

You’ll land outside the building, and the Teacher will finally stop looking for you. Once the coast is clear, jump out of this bin and move through the alley. Cross the plank to the other side.

Continue down the alley and you’ll reach a dumpster. With Six’s help, push it forward so the lid closes, then hop on the TV, jump on the lid, and jump to the next area. Continue moving forward and, in an alley, you’ll find glitching remains by a paper boat.

After doing that, head through the broken door on the right. Here, you’ll find Six’s classic yellow raincoat on the ground. After she puts it on, head through the next door and you’ll wind up in a tailor’s shop.

Then, jump out the window and continue outside. There’s another dumpster here by a gate that Six will boost you onto. From here, climb on top of the TV and onto the roof. Drop down into the area on the opposite side of the gate and knock down the wooden board so Six can come through.

She’ll run up to the vent on the right, so follow her over there and help her open it. Then, crawl through. This will lead you into your next area: The Hospital.
