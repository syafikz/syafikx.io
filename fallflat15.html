Human Fall Flat Level 15 - Golf

Level 15 - Golf

Text walkthrough:

Two more updates have been released for this game, so let's get through them again, beginning with Golf! Once again, you can find this one in the Extra Dreams section from the main menu. Compared to some of the previous updates, this is thankfully a far less complex level than usual and if you've got all the other achievements this game has to offer, you should have very little difficulty with this and get another four achievements in no time.

As you drop into the course, straight ahead of you, you will see a golf ball on a tee, and this is where we can immediately get our first achievement. Using only a golf club, we need to knock (or putt) the ball into the hole to the right of it in three strokes or less, meaning you cannot touch it with the club more than three times. This is a lot simpler than it sounds as you only have to knock it a short distance.

If you drop down to the ball, you can grab a club from the rack on the left. Now I would put yourself at a right angle to the hole, and whack the ball towards it with the side of your club (careful not to touch it with your body at all or you'll void the achievement!). The ground goes slightly downward towards the hole, so a single hit should get it pretty close unless your aim was way off, and maybe even straight into the hole if you're lucky. If you think the ball is going to roll past the hole, try and knock it in quickly before that happens. Once you've successfully done it in 3 strokes or less, you'll unlock:

 

Birdie

Using ONLY the golf club, putt the ball in 3 strokes or less

If you mess this up, just reload a checkpoint and you're right there to try again. It's very simple, so it shouldn't take you too long at all. When the ball's in the hole, it will power up the windmill next to it. Squeeze through its door and jump off the wooden platform to get to a black button. Step on this and you'll get hit by a giant golf club onto the next island. Head through the doorway here into another area.

You'll be overlooking some giant tees from this point. Follow them down the ledges (no need to jump on the top of them) and climb up the short brown wall ahead. You'll quickly come to another hole next to a windmill, a red lever and a wall where you'll be able to see a golf ball rolling. The red lever controls the platforms in the wall that allow the golf ball to roll down to the bottom. To get it down there, tilt the platforms down to the left, then back down to the right after the ball is off the first platform, then quickly left again before it rolls off the last two platforms. Done right, the ball will make it into the path at the bottom and roll into the hole, powering the next windmill.

Don't head through its door this time though, as we are going to take an alternate path for another achievement. You're going to want to grab onto the blades of the windmill to get on top of it and go over the top of the cave. The best way to do this is to try and grab onto the right side of a blade as it comes down to you. When it takes you up to the top of the windmill, you'll now be on the left side and in a good position to try and drop down to climb onto the top, or even just drop/swing straight onto it. Once you're there, drop down onto the grassy area up here. Head forwards, past the giant hole in the ground, and carefully drop down off the end onto the ledge below with a wooden platform. As you land down there, you'll unlock:

 

Bogey (1 Over Par)

Take the alternate route above the cave

Now hanging in front of that wooden platform is a golf club. Jump and grab onto this to swing and drop down into the next grassy area. To your left here, you'll see a black button with a golf club and a ball on a tee by it, and a moving hole beyond it. I think you understand what we need to do here. It takes a bit of timing to get the ball into the hole, but what worked for me was waiting until the hole stops on the right, then stepping onto the button just as it starts moving back to the left. The ball respawns if you miss, so again, it shouldn't take too long. This again opens the way through the next windmill.

On the other side of the windmill, you'll see another giant tee with a golf club swinging back and forth across it. Wait until it has just swung past, then quickly jump over to climb onto the tee before jumping back off to the roof of the golf cart below. Drop down onto the grass beyond, then climb up the wall ahead of you. To your left, there's another tee to jump across to get over the gap.

Time for an extremely easy achievement. You'll see a red golf cart here which we can actually drive. Feels like we haven't done any vehicle driving in a while, huh? Go ahead and hop into this, then push the red lever forwards to go straight off the edge. Once you respawn, easy as that, you'll unlock:

 

Mulligan

Drive the golf cart off the edge of the level

That's also the last miscellaneous achievement in the level, so now we just have to finish it. To progress, get in the red golf cart again and move it carefully forwards so that it's in between the other brown golf carts. Now jump into the golf cart on your right and climb up onto the grassy platform with some boxes on it. Turn around from here and you can use the roofs of the golf carts to get across to the other side. Ignore all the balls here; simply hop into another red golf cart and drive straight forwards to go down a hill, crashing through some boxes at the bottom.

Get out and drop down to the lower area if the cart didn't make it there, then head through the giant cave to your right. To your left on the other end is a wooden platform overlooking some more giant tees with clubs swinging across them. The pattern of these might look a little more daunting to get past, but it's really not an issue. Just wait on the first tee until the first club has just swung past, then quickly jump to the second tee and over to grab onto the edge of the third tee. You can safely wait here until the second club has swung past, then climb up and jump over to the dirt on the other side.

Head left here to find a black button, a lever and another course in the wall that you'll have to roll a ball through. This is basically a slightly more complex version of the puzzle from earlier, but again, it's very simple to work out. When the moving platform won't be in the way of the ball, step on the button to hit it onto the platform to the right, then as the moving platform is moving down to the bottom, when it's about halfway down, push the lever left to roll the ball down onto it. As it's about to stop at the top again, push the lever right to get the ball into the hole.

Climb up the ledges to the left from here to get to the next windmill. However, there's nothing in the door but a sign telling us to go up, so up we shall go. Use the same strategy as we did to climb the windmill earlier and you should be able to get onto the top of this windmill easily enough. Carefully jump off onto the wooden platform nearby when the blades aren't in the way, and follow the rocky path around to a hanging golf club in front of a crashed golf cart. Jump over to hang from this club, then swing back and forth to get enough momentum to land on the roof of the cart before jumping to the next wooden platform.

Climb up some more to the left to enter into... a volcano? This is a change of scenery. Jump along the platforms here, then swing off another golf club to the ledge beyond. Up the next set of ledges, you'll find a geyser. Look up to locate the exit sign (slightly to your left when facing the geyser), then face the direction of the sign and step onto the geyser to get blown up to it and make your way back outside. To your left are some ramps leading up to two giant golf balls. Hop into either one of these and give it a push to get it rolling down the hill, then hold onto the ball and you'll roll straight into the exit hole! That's the level over, and you'll unlock:

 

Hole in One

Complete "Golf"
