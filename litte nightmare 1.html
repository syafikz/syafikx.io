The Wilderness

The game starts off with Mono waking up in a forest by a TV. This is the Wilderness, and it's a truly terrifying place to be. Head right until you reach a small gap. Jump across it, and continue forward until you reach a big tree. Head up to the small vent at the base of it, lift it up, and head inside.

 

This will take you to the opposite side of the tree, where you’ll see a bag filled with bodies hanging from a separate tree. Keep moving forward.

Once you’ve jumped off the log, you’ll come across a tripwire on the ground. Jump over it, or you can sneak around the sides of it. You’ll then come across a box standing up. In order to reach the next cliff, you’ll have to push it to the base of it then climb up.

 

 

After doing this, crawl through to your next area. A tree trunk will come flying towards you, so you’ll have to sprint to escape it. Run down the hill until the trunk is stopped by some other trees.

Once you’re down the hill, you’ll reach an area with a noose hanging above you. Directly below it is a tree trunk that you can crawl through to reach the top. Then, jump towards the noose and swing over to the next area.

Next, you’ll come across a lift with a box attached. In order to get to the other side, you need to turn the lever until the top of the box matches up with the planks. Then, run towards it, grab on, climb on top, and jump to the other side.

 

Once across, you’ll see another bridge, but it’s completely broken. Instead of making a run for it, jump down on the side of the bridge and you’ll land on a box. Then, jump all the way down. From here, head right to find a secret area that will lead you to glitching remains as well. Then, head back out and you can grab the planks on the right to climb up the other side.

On the other side, you’ll drop down into an area with more shoes. Pick one up while you’re down there because you’ll need it in a bit.

This next area has a trap on the ground, so throw your shoe in the middle of it to set it off. Then, use the log that dropped on the opposite side to climb up.

Once you drop down into the next area, be wary of more traps on the ground. Once you navigate around them and hop over the log, you’ll find another one with a stick inside it. Grab onto it and wiggle it around to get the stick out.

After getting the stick out, make your way to the trap in front of the fallen, hollowed out tree trunk. Throw it down to set off the trap, then head inside the trunk.

Once you’re out of the trunk, you’ll find another stick at the bottom. But first, head left underneath the trunk. You'll find another secret area that will lead you to glitching remains. Then, head back out.

There are several traps hidden under leaves, so hit the leaves right before walking through them. This will clear space for you to move while also setting off any traps in your way. Once you reach the stone elevated above the leaves, jump to the other side.

Now you have pinecones to use. Chuck them to different spots around this area to clear out the traps. Once you safely reach the stone, you’ll have to jump to the tree trunk and carefully make your way across it. Then, jump to the planks across from you. Be careful, though, as there’s a trap right beneath you!

Tip: If you want, you can even go back and grab more pinecones to set off the rest of the traps.

Once you’re on the other side, you’ll come across a house. This is the Hunter's house. Go up the stairs and head inside. You can’t open the door, so head over to the lit-up window, crawl up the box in front of it, and head inside.

You’ll wind up in the kitchen. Once you’re done poking around in here, head through the door on the opposite side that’s opened a crack.

This will take you into the hallway, but before heading into the room across from you, head down the hallway and take a left to go into a different room. Here, you’ll find a collectible hat on the ground. Then, backtrack and head through the door with light coming out of it.

This will take you down to the basement. At the bottom of the stairs to your left, you’ll find a small child behind a door. It’s Six! Now, you have to open the door. So head back and enter the room on the other side.

Here, you’ll find an axe. Jump up to grab it and wiggle it out of place, then drag it over to the other door and break it open. Approach Six, but she’ll run away, so chase her up the stairs, down the hall, and into the room opposite of where you found the hat.

This will lead you into a dining room with some very creepy taxidermied people sitting around the table. Ignore them, as they won’t come after you, and continue following Six.

You’ll then end up in a different room with a lever you’ll need to pull. Unfortunately, neither you nor Six can reach it alone, so you’ll have to work together. Six will call you over, then grab onto her hands and she'll push you up to the lever. Once you have a hold on it. She’ll pull your feet and a ladder will drop down leading to the attic.

Head up the ladder and into the attic. Here, you’ll have to find the key to open the door in the room below. Once you’re up there, push the case in front of you towards the dresser so you can climb over it.

The key is in the middle of the room, but it requires turning a crank to the left of it to get it down. Unfortunately, it’s missing the crank. So, head to the smaller dresser to the left of this spot. Stand near it, and Six will offer to help you over.

Once you’re over, head down the plank and under the shelves. You’ll find the crank in the hand of a taxidermied lady, so you’ll have to pull it out of there. Once you’ve got it, head back to Six. She’ll put the crank in the slot and start turning it.

Then, you need to climb the rope on the sack to the right of the key. Once you’re at the top, jump over to the key, grab onto it, and swing back and forth to drop it.

If you downloaded the Day One edition of Little Nightmares 2, you'll notice a Nome appear after getting the key that will run up to the dresser on the left. This is part of The Nome's Attic pre-order DLC, and If you want to follow it, you'll get a collectible Nome hat. You can find full directions on finding the Nome Hat below, otherwise just skip down to The Wilderness, Continued.

The Nome's Attic DLC - Finding the Nome Hat

Climb back over and you’ll find the Nome climbing up the drawers across from the taxidermied lady. Follow it up into this next area and it’ll hide in a case. Open it, and it’ll run to the right and hide in a box.

Use the shoe on the floor to hit the box, and it’ll run back out and into a drawer to the left of the case. Once you open that, it’ll run again and hide by the chair. So climb up on the chair and jump on its arm rest, then it’ll run to the picture on the floor.

It’ll move the picture to reveal a hole in the wall. Climb through and you’ll end up in a darkly-lit room. Thankfully, the Nome will light some candles for you. Make your way through the room, and you’ll notice the Nome can’t move much further. Across from where it’s standing, you’ll find a stack of crates you can grab onto and move towards it.

Once it’s on there, push the Nome over to the other side. Keep walking to the right and you’ll reach a gate. The Nome will open it for you, then head inside. You’ll find a switch on the wall. Pull it, and all of the lights will turn on. Now, head back out and you’ll find a drawer you can pull out.

Pull it all the way out, jump on it, then climb up. Follow the Nome’s pathway, but you’ll have to take a slightly different route. At the point below, climb up and over near the candle on the top shelf, and continue making your way over.

The Nome will run through a hole in the wall, but will leave behind a collectible hat for you. So grab it, then follow the Nome through the hole. Be careful not to go too far here, otherwise you’ll fall. Jump forward and grab on to the panels in the wall and slowly make your way down.

At the bottom of this section, jump to the opposite side to continue heading down. Once you’re at the bottom, pull the plank off the hole to your left and crawl through. This will lead you back to the attic with Six.

The Wilderness, Continued

Now, head down to the locked door and unlock it. This will lead you outside. Head up to the outhouse first, though. Six will boost you up to open it, and you'll find glitching remains inside. Continue following the path into the shed. You’ll have to push a crate towards the window in order to reach it, but it’ll stop after a couple pushes, so you’ll have to jump from there to the window. Now, you’ll meet your first antagonist: the Hunter.

Sneak around him, avoiding any items on the floor, until you reach a piece of wood you’ll have to move in order to get out. Unfortunately, this will make a lot of noise, and he’ll instantly be alerted to your presence. So, get ready to run.

He’ll start shooting at you with his shotgun, so you’ll have to quickly move behind boxes for cover. Once you reach a drop off, jump down and quickly run left to hide. Once he starts walking away, Six will hold out her hand for you to grab, that way the two of you stick together.

Then, head towards the tall grass and duck down. This will help you stay hidden from the Hunter. If he shines his light on you, stop moving and stay hidden until he moves it somewhere else.

Once you reach the end of the tall grass, you’ll have to make a break for it to reach the hole at the bottom of the tree. Hold on tight to Six and book it. Once you’re in there beneath the tree, head right until you reach a light shining through, then exit out.

You’ll soon reach a broken bridge. Grab on to the end of the rope and pull to lift up the broken part. That way, Six can jump over. She’ll run a little further away, so just call her and she’ll come back to you. Then, jump over to her and grab onto her hand.

On this side of the bridge, you’ll see some cages stacked on top of each other. Climb up them, and you’ll see something in a cage in front of you. Jump over to it and climb on top, then start jumping until it breaks and hits the ground. Here, you’ll find another collectible hat. Now, head back over to Six and continue forward.

You’ll hear the Hunter again, so get ready to run. You’ll have to move quickly, because a crow will alert him to your presence. Run over to the building and start climbing up. Once inside, continue running and follow Six out the window and down the awning into the water.

He’ll come back out, but go backwards to hide under the dock. Once he walks away, start swimming. Once you reach the next dock, move up towards the one panel and duck under it.

On the other side, you’ll have to swim to the next tree stump. So start making your way over, but duck underwater whenever his light shines on you. When you reach the next hiding spot, climb up and go towards the tree with a heart on it. With Six’s help, push it over and use it as cover to navigate the water.

Be quick about jumping back in the water, though. Once you make it all the way over, climb up the planks and get ready to run through this next area, as the crows will once again draw attention your way.

Run inside the shed and grab the shotgun off the wall. With Six’s help, lift it up, aim it at the Hunter, and shoot.

Once the Hunter’s dead, head right out the window. You’ll find a broken door in the water. Push it in, and this will take you to the next area.

You’ve now landed on the beaches of the Pale City. Before entering through the door on the right. Head to the TV stuck in the sand. There’s glitching remains here. After doing that, head through the open doorway.

In this next room, continue moving forward to the open hole in the wall. You’ll now be in an alleyway, but continue moving forward and through the open window. This will take you into what looks like a diner, then keep moving forward to your next location.
