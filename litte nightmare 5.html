The Signal Tower

 

Once you’ve taken down the Thin Man, you’ll be taken to the Signal Tower’s entrance. When the door opens, head inside toward the purple light. This will lead you back into the long hallway that was seen before from the TVs. Head straight and through the big door.

The purple light will be in front of you, then that door will close and another will open on the right. Head through it and you’ll appear at the base of some stairs. Follow those up to the next doorway.

From here, you’ll be led into a hallway. This area is a little tricky, since going through the right door will lead you back through the door on the left. The key here is to find the correct pattern so that you’ll hear the music coming out of the door on the left. Pay close attention to the sound here, but the pattern will be

right door, right door, left door, left door.

In this next hallway, head to the second doorway where the door is bent up and you’ll hear the music coming out. Then, in this next area, head through the door on the left.

 

Go up the staircase until you reach your next area. Again, this is the same kind of music puzzle. Push against the closed door next to the open one on the left. This will open the one on the back right. Pay close attention to the music coming out of the doorways again. This time around, the pattern is

the door in the back left, then the back right door, then the door you came in on the right.

Continue up the stairs. In this next room, you’ll have several doors to choose from. First, push down the plank in the middle to bridge a path between the two areas. Then,

Head up the staircase and into the next hallway. On the far left you’ll see a door slightly open with music coming out of it. Push it open and head inside.

In this room, you’ll find a creepy, and very large, version of Six with a music box. She’ll take the music box and start to move back. Follow her, and once you’re right in front of her, call out to her.

She’ll move to the middle of the room with the music box, leaving a giant mallet available for you to grab onto on the left. Carry it over, and smash the music box. Unfortunately, the monster version of Six wasn’t too happy with that, and she’ll now start chasing you.

Run through the halls and once you land in the safe room after crawling through the vent space, hide underneath the table. She’ll start searching through the room, but will eventually leave through the door. Now, head out the same door right behind her.

In this next area, you’ll see an axe stuck in a door. Grab it and smash open the bottom of it to go through. You’ll find Six in here with the music box again, but you can’t smash it yet. First, you have to lure her away from it.

Grab the axe again, then call her name and run through the portal. While she’s distracted on the left side, head to the music box and smash it. Once you’re back on your feet, grab the axe again and smash open the door.

Be prepared to do the same thing here. While holding the axe, distract Six and head through the portal. However, this time, head through the left portal to the right, call her again, then head back again.

Next, head up the ramp to the second portal on the left. Call her name then jump to the right, head to the music box, and smash it again.

Once you’re back on your feet again, head right until you hit another door with an axe in it. Take the axe out, and smash open the door. This time, your axe is over by Six. So, first, call her over right as you’re about to go through the portal on the left.

Then, from the right, jump up to grab the axe and head through the first portal on the right again because she’ll see you. Then, from the left, call her over again and head through. When you’re back on the right, head up to the second portal, call her over again, and head through. Then, run all the way to the music box and hit it.

Now, pick up the axe and make your way towards Six and the music box one last time. Call her name out again so she moves her hands, then hit it. Six will try crawling toward it again, so give it one last hit.

Six is back to normal now, but unfortunately, whatever’s unleashed in the Signal Tower is coming after you now. So follow her and run. As the Signal Tower begins to crumble, you’ll have to jump and catch her hand.
