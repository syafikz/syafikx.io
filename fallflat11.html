Human Fall Flat Level 11 - Steam

Level 11 - Steam

Text Walkthrough:

Welcome to the next update level, Steam! This is another relatively long level in comparison to the rest of the game, but it is not nearly as long as Dark nor does it have any particularly challenging sections. The miscellaneous achievements are also pretty easy, so just take your time and you'll be fine.

As you start the level, turn around behind you to find a metal seesaw. Grab it and move it to the left up against the wall. Go along it onto the metal grate, then drop down onto the platform straight ahead. You’ll see another seesaw ahead; grab it and move it underneath the truck that is dumping red barrels so that one can drop down to you. If it doesn’t roll into place, get the barrel up against the wall directly behind you / across from the truck in between the black and yellow strips, then climb up onto it from the left side and jump straight up to the ledge ahead. Now head down the ramp and you’ll see blue piping to your right; drop down there, being careful not to knock the pipe, and pull the red lever by the gate to open it. Now grab the pipe on the other side of the gate then turn back around to see a boiler with a pipe blowing air out of it. Connect your pipe to that pip and It should pop into place with the smoke now blowing through that pipe. Grab the middle pipe behind you (it’s a lighter shade of blue than the others) and pull it out of place, then put it in the gap in the pipes on the other side as before. This will extend the pipeline past the gate, and you can now head back up the ramp, drop down the other side, and pull the lever by the green light on the left to open the gate to the next area.

As you walk in, you’ll see another barrel on a raised platform, and to your left will be a seesaw contraption with a pipe piece at the top of it. Climb up to the barrel, then push it to the left following the yellow arrows. For some reason, I had difficulty consistently getting the barrel to make it over the gap to the ground below. What eventually worked for me was pushing it off the edge, then dropping slightly behind it and pushing into it as I fell into the gap which prevented it from rolling backward off the edge and kept it there for me when I respawned. Continue to push the barrel straight in a line between the black and white strips. It should roll over an indent in the floor and push the metal seesaw up. Now head up the ramp to the left and climb on the raised blocky section of wall near the seesaw, and from here, grab onto the end of the seesaw nearest to you. You might need to adjust the position of the barrel to get the seesaw within jumping distance, but once you’ve grabbed it, you’ll pull it down which should knock the blue pipe piece up top down to your level. Grab it before it has a chance to roll off the map, then go back up the ramp and connect it to the gap in the pipeline near the red platform with an opening above it. Once the green light is lit, use the red lever by the platform to send it about halfway up the wall so you can climb onto it and up through the opening.

Straight ahead of you in this area is another seesaw. Jump and grab onto the end closest to you and drop off on the metal grate below. Jump onto the platform behind you and to your right and climb up some ledges to find the seesaw has now formed a bridge across the gap. Pull it as far over to the right as it can go, towards the broken circular structure with a fire inside. It should be in line with a raised platform by that structure. Run across the seesaw and jump over to this platform before the seesaw tips too far down, then pull the planks out of the doorway. Grab one of these planks and head inside the structure, then place the plank inside the fire to light it. Head back out of the doorway, drop down and leave the plank here. Get back over to the other side of the gap, then grab the seesaw, drop on the grate as before and head back up again. The seesaw will now be laying on this side again. Walk across it and stand on the other end, then jump off to the ground. Your jump should force the seesaw down, spinning it around to lay back on this side. Grab the lit plank and head back across the seesaw again, jumping off at the end. Open the hatch of the boiler by where we climbed up into this area and place the plank inside, powering the gate to the next area. Head over the seesaw one last time and pull the red lever to open the gate, then go on through.

Immediately as you head in, you’ll see a large red plank to your right propped up on the edge of this platform. Line up with the middle of it and walk straight into it to push it down below; you should land on it and it will bridge a gap below. Pull it away from that gap, though, and get it towards the one to the right which has the barrel repeatedly falling through it. Wait for the barrel to pass, then quickly push the plank across the middle of the gap to catch it when it falls again. Once the barrel is on the plank, push it across to the other side and down the small ramp to get it up against the wall, then climb onto it and up onto the ledges to your right.

Ahead of you, you’ll see two metal structures, both of which are holding a pipe piece. Simply pull away the legs of each structure out to cause them to collapse and move the pipes away from the debris. Now head up the ramp by the blue fence and climb over the fence to the other side. As you drop down, turn around to see a ladder propped up against the wall. Try to climb it. It will fall apart and you'll unlock:

 

Whoops!

Now turn to your left and pull out the T-shaped pipe piece by the large boiler. Turn around and grab the corner of the S-shaped pipe bend closest to you. You’ll pull out a small corner piece. Place it in the space by the boiler that the T-shaped piece was just in and after a few seconds, a piece of the boiler will fly off, smashing open the gate to the previous section. Head through the gate and carry the two pipe pieces from earlier into this section. Remove the pipe by the red lever as well. You should now have two T-pieces and two corner pieces. Use these to form a square by placing the T-pieces at both ends of the pipelines, and the corner pieces in the middle to connect them together. Now pull the red lever and the gate to the next area will open. Here is a screenshot of the positioning of the pipes to guide you:

 

 

There’s another achievement to be gained in this area for getting to the big wheel without using the crane controls, so ignore the piping ahead of you as you head through the gate and turn to your left instead to find two red barrels. Push the one that is on its side into the glass behind it to break through. Grabbing the flat circular sides makes it easier to maneuver. When the glass is broken, you’ll then need to get enough of the glass pieces out of the way to pull the barrel through into this area. You can just throw them off the side if you want as they won’t respawn like other objects. Once you’ve gotten the barrel over the glass, push it by the metal/tin overhang across from where the glass was (it has a tree on top of it), then climb up onto the barrel and onto the overhang. It can be tough to get on top of it, so try to use the thin wooden supports of the overhang to keep the barrel in place and prevent it from rolling about. From here, climb up onto the raised section with the tree, then grab the end of the chain on the ground by it and walk off the edge towards the big wheel. Jump as the chain swings out towards the other side and you should land safely over there. You can try and swing back and forth to gain momentum if you want, but I found I’d actually lose momentum and fall short of the other side if I did it that way. Shortly after making it over, you'll unlock:

 

Walk the Plank

In this new area, head around to the right side of the large red box to find a seesaw laying against it. Pull it from the side so that it tips over the other way to rest against the stone plateau instead. Climb up the ledges up onto the plateau, then run towards the seesaw, jump before you hit it, and grab onto it. It will tip back over onto the red box, and you can jump forwards up the seesaw to end up on top of the box yourself. Grab the pipe piece here, drop off the box to the right and connect the piece between the pipe coming out of the box that is nearest the seesaw and the pipe going into the generator truck thing by the wheel. The wheel should now start spinning. You can get another easy achievement here by grabbing the underside of the wheel so you spin with it. Keep holding in and you will spin all the way up to its top and back down again. After you’ve held on for three revolutions of the wheel, you'll unlock:

 

Get Dizzy

Now grab onto the wheel again and as you near the top, let go and aim for the platform to the side with the plus-shaped pipe piece. Jump back down with it in hand and place it into the middle of the three pipes coming out of the box and the pipe going into the generator. You’ll need to move the straight pipe piece at the farthest pipe from the seesaw and place the corner pipe there to connect it all together. Again, here is a picture of what this looks like:

 

 

This will now cause the wheel to get overcharged. It will start to spark and after a few seconds, it will roll out of place and crash straight through the large tower-like structure. Head in there and simply drop through the giant hole inside to finish this level. You'll unlock:

 

Under Pressure
