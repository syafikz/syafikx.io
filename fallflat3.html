Human Fall Flat Level 3 - Carry

Level 3: Carry

 

Text Walkthrough:

Welcome to level 3, and I'm sure as you can tell by the name of the level, you're going to be doing a lot of carrying. I'm going to give you some good news right off the bat as I earned the achievement for walking 1 km in this level, so odds are you will too. And if not this one, then level 4 for sure.

 

Tourist

Travel 1km

 
1 guide

 

Now back to the fun stuff, first thing you need to do is pick up the box close to you and place it on the button. Doing this will open the door to the next area. Now we are going to be setting up for the Tower achievement, which requires us to stack all 4 boxes in the level on top of each other. If the text is too hard to follow, definitely use the video walkthrough for it. The level is broken up into 4 areas, with the first area being the area you spawn and the last section being the area with the exit door. Grab the box on the ledge to your left and carry it back into the area where you spawned. Now grab the box that is currently on the button and take it off. Grab the box you took from the second area and place it on the button.

Now before we continue we're gonna take a little pause for another miscellaneous achievement. Place the box that was previously on the button in the middle of the door heading to the second area. Head back to the button and take the box off of it and place it beside it. You'll notice the other box is holding the door open. Jump on top of the box stuck in the door and keep walking forward until you limbo through the door, unlocking:

 

It's stuck

Jam a door and go through it in "Carry"

 
1 guide

 

Now go back and place the box you took off the button back onto it to open the door again. Take the box that was stuck in the door and bring it into the second area. Now go and grab the box off the button in the starting area and, while holding it, jump off the edge. You'll fall and respawn with the box in the second area.

Going to make a note of this here so you don't think you can do this with any box. The reason this box respawned here is because this is it's original spawn. That's why we swapped the original box on the button with this one.

Now that we have that all straightened out, you need to place the box from the first area on the button in this area. This will open the door to the third area and make sure you bring the box that spawns the second area into this third area. Once you're here, bring this box onto the lift and push the button on the lift to raise it up. Once you're up top, place the box on the button to open the door to area four. In this area, you need to drop down and grab the box. Carry it over to the lift and push the button on the lift to raise it up. Once you're at the top, carry the box onto the red box car here and place it at the far left end of it. You then need to grab onto the wall and walk to the right to move the box car to the left. Once it's over far enough, grab the box again and jump over to the ledge and head back into the third area.

Now that you're back in the third area, place the box from the fourth area on the ground by the ledge that has the door to the second area. Now you need to place the box from the third area on top of the box from the fourth area. Once you manage this, jump and pull yourself up onto the ledge leading to the second area, and grab the box from the third area off the top of the box from the fourth area. Once you do this, carry this box back into the second area and drop it. Take the box that is on the button in the second area and drop it somewhere away from the box you just brought from the third area. Place the box from the third area on the button, and carry the box from the first area into the third area and place it anywhere. Now take the box on the button in the second area and jump off. You should land back in the third area and have all four boxes in the third area.

Now you just need to stack all the boxes. Grab two boxes and stack them on top of each other on ground level up against the ledge that has the button to open the door to the fourth area. Push the lift button on the ground level to call the lift back down and grab the only box left on ground level, take it onto the lift, and push the button on the lift to take it up to the top. Place the box that you brought with you on the lift on top of the two boxes that are already stacked, then take the last box off the button for the fourth area and place it on top of all three boxes. Once you do this successfully, you'll unlock:

 

Tower

Stack all 4 boxes in the level "Carry"

Now for the easy part, finishing the level. Take one of the boxes off your beautiful tower and place it back on the button to open the door to area 4. Grab one of the other boxes from your tower and bring it with you through the door and place it on the button just in front and to the right of you as soon as you walk into area 4. This will open the door. Now simply hop across the box car or take the lift, walk through the door, and as you're falling you'll unlock:

 

Don't Get a Splinter!

Complete "Carry"
