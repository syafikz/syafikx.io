Human Fall Flat Level 10 - Dark

Level 10 - Dark

Text Walkthrough:

Welcome to the second update level, Dark! This is by far the longest level in the game, but aside from a handful of challenging sections, it's the sheer length of the level that is difficult here more than anything, so don't worry too much. One of the level's miscellaneous achievements requires completing the entire level without moving any of the planks that are barring up wooden doors which this walkthrough will help you to get. However, there are reports of this achievement glitching if you get too close to the doors, regardless of whether you move the planks or not, so whenever I point out the location of one of these doors, keep well clear of them to be on the safe side and prevent having to replay this level all over again.

As you start the level, you'll find yourself in a house with a crate to your left and a door straight ahead with a slanted ledge above it. This is one of the barred doors that you are not allowed to open, so stay away from it and instead, grab the crate and drop it underneath the window behind you so you can climb up to and through it. Turn right on the other side and carefully jump over to the small green platform, then turn back around and climb up onto the stone platform sticking out from the wall. Climb the wall to your left and you’ll drop down into the upper level of the house. Grab the axe here and carefully get it on top of the wall you just climbed over, then climb onto the wall yourself, grab the axe and carefully drop back down to the stone platform and the small green platform below it. Head right from here, past the house and down the ramp towards the tree. Grab the handle of the axe with both hands and swing at this tree; after a couple of successful hits, it will fall down. Push it towards the gap with the burning torches on the other side, guiding it between the two stumps to create a bridge, then carefully cross over. A couple of quick jumps should get you across it safely.

Now head up the ramp on your left and grab onto the cage at the end to swing around the corner, then drop down to the grassy platform below. Climb up two wooden platforms to come to a well with a grate covering it and a hanging basket on the right with a rock inside it. There are more rocks strewn around the well. Grab two of them and drop them inside the basket also. The grate will then be lifted from the well, allowing you to drop inside.

You’ll see the clock tower as you move forward, and this is where you have the opportunity for the first achievement of the level. You need to climb this clock tower from the set of stairs at its bottom right up to the top ledge in 40 seconds or less. This is not as tough as it might sound, though, as the timing is not overly tight and as you can reload the checkpoint just before the clock tower if you mess up, it shouldn’t take too long to get a clean run. Make your way up the two sets of wooden stairs, then head left towards the set of rocks and as you climb the third set of wooden stairs by the clock tower itself, your time to climb up to the top starts.

Head around the corner at the top of the stairs and climb up these two platforms. For this next corner, you can either jump around and try and grab the ledge to the left as you go around the corner or jump straight ahead and grab onto the tree instead, then angle your camera left and jump off and grab the ledge that way. Both methods can be tricky to pull off, but you’ll get them with practice and the rest of the run is easier once you’re safely on this ledge. Next, head through the window and try and jump along the thin ledge on the left wall to get over to the opposite window, as it will save you wasting time climbing up into it. Then climb up another platform on your left outside, and another around the corner, then onto the chimney. There’s one last platform and then you’re up onto the top ledge. Stop once you’ve reached this final ledge and wait for a couple of seconds; if you were quick enough, you'll unlock:

 

Tick Tock

If not, you can reload and try again. In the event that the game did not give you a checkpoint right before the clock tower though, what you can also do is complete the following puzzle which will give you another checkpoint. Reload this and you'll now have the correct checkpoint at the bottom of the clock tower. You'll be safe to keep trying for the achievement from there.

Now follow the ledge around into the clock room where you’ll find two cranks. Turn both of these cranks as far anti-clockwise as they will go. Once that’s done, the bell will sound and a piece of it will fall. (This will give you that checkpoint which you can reload from which will place you at the bottom of the clock tower.) Drop through the now broken floor to find the piece at the bottom, grab it and then turn right from the clock tower and drop down the ledges back towards the lit torches to end up where you were earlier. Leave the bell piece here by the giant furnace in the middle. Now we want to head to the other side of this area instead, straight ahead from where we just dropped down. Climb up the steps, then up onto the grassy ledge on your right and head around the corner into a cave. Follow the rail track inside down to the left where you will see a minecart at the end that is blocked in by boulders; pick each of them up and move them out of the way. You can drop them in the alcove to the right or take them back to the start of the cave.

Once the track is clear, grab the minecart by the handle and pull it all the way back, then tip it over and grab one of the pieces of coal inside. Follow the track back to the central area outside, then place the coal inside the furnace in the center. Pick the bell piece up and drop it in the hole at the top of the furnace, using the stairs by its side to climb up to the top. Now you need to grab one of the nearby torches; go for the one that is inside a partially broken base, as it is easier to slide out. Place the torch inside the furnace to light it up and melt the bell piece; it will form into a key which will appear on the mold by the side of the furnace. If this doesn't work, you might need to go back and grab another piece of coal from the minecart to place inside the furnace. Once you've got it, grab the key and place it inside the giant keyhole on the gate behind the furnace before twisting it to unlock the gate to the next area.

Head to your right as you enter this area and climb up the grassy ledges. You’ll end up on a stony surface and find another minecart straight ahead; hop in and ride it down the track. Quickly jump out when you make it back down to grassy land at the bottom though, as the cart will crash straight through the fence at the end and plummet off the map. Head along the grass to the left and you’ll see a wooden beam in front of you. Grab onto it and swing, jumping off at the farthest point of your swing to land on the grass below. You’ll have to do this with another two wooden beams; the second beam is particularly tough to reach, so try to jump at the last possible moment in order to grab it.

After that, you’ll have some more grassy ledges to climb, then a set of wooden stairs to your left that leads to a giant wheel machine next to a house. You now need to enter this house to retrieve two batteries that can be charged with the machine. Head over towards the machine, then go past it on the left, then up the stairs to your left to enter the house. The stairs are broken, so you’ll have to jump a gap to climb up to the top. Then turn left and head straight, jumping a gap to go over a stone wall into another room. You’ll see the first battery lying right here, but the second barred-up door we need to avoid is here, meaning you need to keep away from that door and instead get the battery back over the wall we just jumped. To do this, grab the crate and push it right in the corner between the wall and the shelf. Then grab one of the barrels in the alcove and stand it upright against the wall by the crate. Now grab the battery, jump up onto the crate, then onto the barrel, and try to throw or drop the battery over the wall. It can be frustrating to do this, as the barrel can be difficult to get standing upright and it can easily topple over when you’re on top of it, but it should hopefully not take too long to get this done.

With the battery over the wall, climb back over and grab the battery on the other side, then head through the door straight ahead and follow the ledge outside around to the right to end back up at the machine. Drop the battery here. Now head back up the stairs into the house, jumping the gap at the top and turning left, but this time, climb the short wall immediately to your left. Go up the stairs to your right outside, then jump around the corner onto another wooden platform, up onto the shelf, and over another wall. Follow this ledge to your right, then climb back over the wall on your right to find the second battery on top of some shelves. Drop down to the right to land back at the top of the stairs, then jump back down outside and place this battery in the charging slot to the right of the machine, making sure that the red and blue ends of the battery are aligned correctly. Now turn the handle clockwise until you hear an electrical zap indicating that the battery is fully charged, then remove it and repeat with the other battery.

Now that both batteries are fully charged, grab each of them and head back past the machine the way we came, going straight ahead towards the elevator in the distance. You need to place each battery in the charging slots on the left and right side of this elevator, again taking care to make sure that the red and blue ends of the battery are aligned correctly. Once they’re both in place, you’ll see the inside of the elevator light up; head on in, and press the button to send it up.

At the top, jump up and go around to the left, climbing up yet more grassy ledges, and head into the large house straight ahead at the top. Inside, to your right, you’ll see a blue cable on the table and a red cable on the floor at the far end. Grab them both and head up the stairs by the table. Upstairs, you’ll find a power outlet, a giant metal object hanging by it from the ceiling and a magnet in the corner by another set of stairs. The door at the top of these stairs is barred up by wooden planks on the other side, so again, keep away from it to prevent possibly voiding the achievement. Grab the magnet and place it underneath the metal object, then connect the cables from the magnet to the outlet to turn the magnet on. It will attract the object from the ceiling, causing the window to the right to swing open. Climb up onto the windowsill and grab onto the rope hanging from the window. You’ll need to swing back and forth on this rope to gain as much momentum as possible so you can land on the platform to your right; angle your camera in that direction to find it, then once you think you can make the jump, let go of the rope and hopefully, you'll make it onto the platform. I found this jump to be the hardest part of the level personally and a real struggle to get past; maybe it was just me, but time after time, I would stop dead in midair after letting go of the rope and fall short of the platform. It seems to work best if you grab closer to the bottom of the rope as you can swing further that way, giving you a crucial extra bit of momentum.

Once you’re on the platform, follow the stairs up (keep away from the wooden door on your left at the top of the stairs as it's another one of the barred doors), climbing another three sets of stairs up into a room with electrically charged equipment, then head through the door straight ahead to drop down into another room. You’ll see a Frankenstein-like monster lying on a slab with the exit symbol on it, but we still have one last chunk of the level left to get through as we now need to retrieve two more batteries to ‘power-up’ the monster and open the exit. However, I can assure you the hardest bits are over and done with, so don’t give up now! Head right into the elevator (keep to the left side by the button inside as you enter to stay away from the barred door from earlier) and push the button to send it down. Once it’s stopped, head out and to the right to find a large wooden pillar that you can push around. Push it to the left until you can’t anymore, sending the platform behind you across the gap.

Time to get those batteries. Head back towards the elevator and drop underneath the cage to the right, then head right and underneath it to find an alcove with the first battery at its end. Grab it and turn back into the main room. Head straight ahead back underneath the cage towards another alcove with a lantern at its end, then jump up to the ledge at the end to find the second battery. Grab both batteries, then follow the wall around to the right as you leave this opening to find a path that goes back up to the top of this room. You’ll come out by the platform from earlier which is now over here; place both batteries inside, then drop off the edge behind and to the right of the platform and you’ll land right by a red cable. Grab it, then head straight ahead and back up the path which will lead you to the platform. Place the cable inside there with the batteries. This is easier said than done though as it can be pretty fiddly getting it to fit in there; try getting one end of the cable into it, then climb in yourself and pull the other end up. If you’re really struggling with it, then you might just want to follow the next paragraph to send the batteries over on their own, then send the platform back so you can put the cable inside on its own to hopefully make it easier.

Once it’s securely in the platform, jump to the small wooden platform to your right, then the next one around the wall, and on to the ledge past that. Jump back onto the cage and swing to land back by the elevator where we started. Head back to the wooden pillar to push it to the right so that the platform is sent to our side. Once it’s back over here, grab the cable and the batteries from within and drop them inside the elevator, then press the button to head back up. Stay by the button to avoid the barred door at the top again. Grab both batteries, leaving the red cable here, then immediately head to your right after leaving the elevator. Look down at the edge and carefully drop down onto the grassy ledge below. You don’t want to drop all the way down here, as if you do, you’ll potentially lose the batteries and may even have to reload the checkpoint and gather the batteries and cable again, which is not fun after everything you’ve gone through to say the least. Follow the wall of the house to the left to drop down onto the roof and follow that around to end back up on the platform from earlier, then take the several sets of stairs back up to the electrical room. Now carefully place each battery into the slots on each side of the room. This can be quite a pain to get them placed correctly, but persevere with it as the level is all but over now.

Once they’re both in place and electricity is sparking through them, drop back down to the monster. Grab the red cable and connect it from the outlet to the slot on the right side of the bed, then connect the blue cable from the outlet to the left side of the bed. Now grab the switch to the right of the outlet and push it up until you hear it click into place, then quickly turn around and grab onto the top of the slab that the monster is lying on. Shortly after, it will fly off, and you should go flying with it. If you mess up, you can reload the checkpoint to get another chance but you will have to place the batteries in their slots again. Once you’re flying with it, hold on and you'll unlock:

Fry Me to the Moon

Unfortunately, you'll have to play through the entire level again to try and unlock it, but at least you can blast through it quicker now that you know what you need to do. No matter what happens, very well done for getting through this level!
