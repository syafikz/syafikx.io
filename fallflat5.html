Level 5 - Demolition

Text Walkthrough:

Once you're done falling, you'll end up in level 5, Demolition. More good news as you start. I earned the achievement for climbing 100m in this level. If you earned it before, congrats! If you don't earn it this level, you'll more than likely earn it in the next one.

 

Climber

Climb 100m

 
1 guide

 

Now on with the level. Pry the wood planks off the door in front of you and move on through. Pick up the fire extinguisher and swing it against the glass to break it. Now we're going to go for a miscellaneous achievement. First thing's first, you need to go to the lever to control the bucket, and move it to it knocks the mattresses beside it out of the way. Now what you need to do is get the bucket to swing back and forth so it breaks the glass on the building to your left. To do this, move the bucket one way, and then when the bucket is at the pinnacle of its swing, immediately start moving it in the opposite direction. So, for example, if the bucket is swinging to the left, once it reaches it's pinnacle of its swing on the left, immediately move it to the right. Keep repeating this until it swings and breaks the glass. Be careful though, don't move the bucket too far to the right, as we need the wall here intact for another achievement. Once the window is broken, grab the fire extinguisher and hop down. Drop the fire extinguisher by the wall on the right and proceed to the window you just broke with the bucket. Use the cheese method to swing your way up and climb into the window. If you can't get the cheese method to work, you can pile up mattresses instead by the window instead and climb up that way. Once inside this building, you'll unlock:

 

Wrong direction

Use the window on your left instead of smashing the wall in "Demolition"

 
1 guide

 

Now during your speedrun you can continue through the other side of this building and skip about half the map, but for the purpose of this walkthrough I'm going to cover the other three areas as well. Climb back out the window and head over to the wall on the right. Pick up your fire extinguisher and start smacking the wall with it until it breaks. Once it does, that will be wall 1/4 for a miscellaneous achievement. In this next area, there is another wall just a bit in front of you on your left. Smack this wall with your fire extinguisher too to break wall 2/4.

In the next area there will be two conveyor platforms, one raised and one lowered, a lever, a glass room with a button in it, and a box. First, go over to the conveyor platform that is raised and pull the yellow pole out from underneath it. Doing this will cause that platform to lower. Climb up to the ledge and walk to the end of the now lowered platform. Jump across to the ledge on the other side and hit the lever beside the glass room, which raises the conveyor platform that was lowered so you can walk back across the gap. Now jump up to the ledge, and grab the box. Carry the box back across the platform, smash the glass with the box, and place the box on the button which opens the door to the next area. Once you're inside the next area, you'll see a platform with two yellow poles underneath it, and a lever to your right with a blue wall behind it. Pull both yellow poles out from underneath the platform and it will lower. Now you need to take a yellow pole and place it horizontally behind the lever and against the blue wall. This will cause the pole to fall on the lever, keeping it held down and keeping the door open for you, so head on through.

This next area contains a lever, a button to your front left, a swinging platform, and a box on the far side across the gap. First thing you need to do is move the swinging platform into the middle of the gap, that way it swings back and forth, with opposite ends touching (or close to) both sides of the gap. Head on down and jump across to the swinging platform and jump from here to the other side. Grab the box and take it back to the swinging platform. When it swings close to you, jump on it with the box. When it swings close to the other side, jump off it with the box and place it on the button. Traverse back over this platform and head into the next area. In this area, there is a wall in front of you to your right, but before we deal with that, we're going to climb up the blue scaffolding to our immediate right. Once you get to the top, you'll see a door that is barricaded. Lift up the barricade and you'll be rewarded with a surprise and an achievement:

 

Surprise! (Avalanche!)

Unleash the boulder gate in "Demolition"

Now you need to grab a rock from the avalanche and smash it against the wall beside the blue scaffolding. Breaking it is wall 3/4. In the next area you'll see a red box car to your left and a wall to your right. Take your trusty rock with you and smash it against the wall until it breaks, resulting in wall 4/4 and a new achievement:

 

Primal

Break 4 walls without using any gadgets in "Demolition"

 
2 guides

 

If you broke a wall using the bucket, the wrecking ball, or the box car, you'll have to start the level over. Continuing on to this next area, we have a yellow platform in front of you. Walk across this and glass will break opposite of you. Climb the stairs over here and you'll see a button in front of you and a platform with a wrecking ball in front and to the right of you. Jump over to this platform and climb up. You now need to push the wrecking ball off the ledge. It's a bit tough so try different maneuvers to get it moving. Once it falls, the yellow platform will be raised up to your level. Head into the building beside you across the walkway. Open the door and grab the box that's inside. Take it out and carry it across the now raised yellow platform, then place it on the button to open the door to the next area.

This next area contains a swinging platform to your left, a yellow generator like object, and a button, so there must be a box around somewhere right?? Head to the right and hop across the two platforms here, then jump from here onto the yellow generator like object. Once on top of it, turn and face the wall that has the door you just came through and you'll see a box on top of a ladder. Raise up your arms and jump towards the ladder. If you're successful, you'll grab onto it, swinging the ladder down and dropping the box. Once the box is down, carry it up the set of stairs and drop it at the top. Next, go down to the lever and bring the swinging platform towards the platform with the box and get it as close as you can. Place the box on the swinging platform then head back down to the lever and move the platform across the gap to the other platform with the set of stairs. Jump across the gap, head up the stairs, and grab the box from the platform and place it on the button, opening the door to the final area.

In here you'll see a forklift type mechanism and a red plank on the ground. Lift up the plank and try your best to place it as far back as you can on the forklift, then use the lever to raise it up. Jump and grab onto the plank and pull yourself up, then jump onto the scaffolding. Climb up the scaffolding until you reach the top, where you'll see a yellow pole. Pull it out and a black swing will drop down. Pick up the yellow pole and jump down where the swing just dropped and you'll break the glass. Pull the lever down here to open the exit gate. Now climb out of this pit and face the exit gate. Jump and grab onto the swing and swing yourself back and forth to gather momentum. Once you feel like you have enough, jump off and fling yourself through the exit and while you're falling you'll unlock:

 

Brute Force

Complete "Demolition"
