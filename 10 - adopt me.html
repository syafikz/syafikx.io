Tips 9

  1. Task Tips. This should be at the top of the list but I almost forgot about it. For Hunger, there are free apples at school at a Teacher's desk. There are also pet bowls at school but only pets can use them. There is also a drinking fountain for pets at the nursery. For Thirst, what I did back then was go inside each open home and see if they have a water cooler so I can swipe some free drinks but looking for them usually isn't worth the time so I recommend buying a water cooler [300 bucks]. If you want to save money, basic cribs are usable for both babies and pets. I think it's also worth investing in a piano [100 bucks]. It cures "bored" so you don't have to go all the way to the playground. For the "sick" task, I recommend just getting some healing apples on the Hospital so you can heal on the go.

  2. Traveling Tips. If you want to go to the main area just click your bag>gifts>+>teleport to gifts display. If you want to go to the Nursery and Baby Shop, just do the same thing in Toys and Pets. You don't need a magic door to go home easily! Just reset your character! But by doing so it will unequip your pet so just equip your pet immediately so you wouldn't miss out on tasks. The starter vehicle I recommend you guys buying is roller skates [300 BUCKS]. Cheap and very helpful to close travel like going to the campsite, etc.
