Level 9 - Aztec

Thanks to an update, Aztec now has achievements. To earn these achievements, it's best to do them in two playthroughs:

-One playthrough to earn the Bird's Eye View and The End achievements

-One playthrough to earn the Rollin' and Clockwork achievements

I would highly suggest doing the Bird's Eye View and The End achievement playthrough as part of your Speedrun playthrough, as you can get to the end of the level in about 5 minutes during this playthrough, and that's how I'm going to order the playthroughs in this section.

Playthrough #1 Text Walkthrough:

Once you start the level, turn around and you'll see two wooden poles wedged under a rock. You'll also notice a massive vertical slab in front of a gap. Go to the wooden poles and pick one up and place it horizontally in front of the vertical slab. Head back and pick up the other pole and place it under the gap of the slab but on top of the wooden pole you just placed horizontally. Jump on the end of the pole wedged under the slab and it should knock the slab over the gap, allowing you to cross. Pick up one of the wooden poles and take it with you across the bridge and you'll come to a door with a hole in it at the bottom. Put the pole into the hole and pull it to try and wedge the door open. Once it's open, head through. Walk forward and you'll see a ledge in between two cages. Climb up on the ledge, then climb up on the higher ledge in between the cages. While facing the wall, hop on the right cage. It will lower and the opposite cage will raise. Once it's lowered a bit, jump back to the platform in the middle and quickly jump to the raised cage and jump across to the platform with a block on it. Move the block beside the tree and climb on top of the block and onto the tree. Climb to the top of the tree and walk straight across and you'll see a brown ledge beside the wall on the right with a light green field beside it in the distance. Jump down and walk across this ledge, then walk across the field to get to another set of trees. Jump up some foliage and walk around to the left and you'll see some more foliage you can climb up. Keep climbing to the top and you'll see a nest at the top. Step in the nest and you'll unlock:

 

Bird's eye view

While you're up here, turn and face the Aztec temple and you'll see a light green field just to the right and below the tree you're on. Jump down onto this field and walk up to the upper part of the temple. Climb the steps and go through the hole at the top and you'll unlock:

Playthrough #2 Text Walkthrough:
Once you start the level, turn around and you'll see two wooden poles wedged under a rock. You'll also notice a massive vertical slab in front of a gap. Go to the wooden poles and pick one up and place it horizontally in front of the vertical slab. Head back and pick up the other pole and place it under the gap of the slab but on top of the wooden pole you just placed horizontally. Jump on the end of the pole wedged under the slab and it should knock the slab over the gap, allowing you to cross. Pick up one of the wooden poles and take it with you across the bridge and you'll come to a door with a hole in it at the bottom. Put the pole into the hole and pull it to try and wedge the door open. Once it's open, head through. Walk forward and you'll see a ledge in between two cages. Climb up on the ledge, then climb up on the higher ledge in between the cages. While facing the wall, hop on the right cage. It will lower and the opposite cage will raise. Once it's lowered a bit, jump back to the platform in the middle and quickly jump to the raised cage and jump across to the platform with a block on it. Move the block beside the tree and climb on top of the block and onto the tree. Climb to the top of the tree and walk straight across and you'll see a brown ledge beside the wall on the right with a light green field beside it in the distance. Jump down and walk across this ledge, then walk across the field to get to another set of trees.

Instead of climbing the tree you did to get Bird's Eye View, just to the right of this tree, you should see a path by some pillars. Walk down this path and drop down and you'll be at the start of the ball puzzle. Facing the lava river, on the left side you'll see a block beside a wooden pole. Move the block to the rear of the wooden pole and right beside it. Move to the front of the wooden pole and jump and grab it to pull it down, then quickly place the block under the wooden pole so it doesn't fall back down. Now you need to do the same with the other side, but you need to find a block to do so. Turn around and hop up a platform, then hop over to the area across the gap and you'll see a wooden plank and a big slab with a gap in the bottom. Slide the wooden plank under the gap as far as it can go, then head around and walk across this plank to grab a block on an island. Grab it and carry it back around the slab back to the gap you previously crossed. Grab the wooden plank from under the slab and try and place it across the gap back to the wooden poles to create a bridge. The way I did it was stood at the back of it and pushed it across. Once it's on the other side, grab the block and walk across back to the wooden poles and place the block under the rear of it the same way you did previously.

Now comes what I think may be the hardest achievement in the game. You need to jump on the ball when it falls and successfully stay on it without falling off until you reach a rocky area on the other side. On either side of the ledge where the wooden poles are, there's a path that you can follow and it will lead you to just above the lava river, in front of where the ball drops. Head up here and wait for the ball to drop. When it does, you need to jump on it and stay on it. The best way to do this is constantly jump and move your body to stay on the very top. It's incredibly difficult and will take practice, but it just feels so random. I did it first try when I earned the achievement, but when I was recording it took me forever. Skip to 16:14 to see my (eventual) successful attempt. Once you safely get to the ledge, you'll unlock:


Rollin'

Keep climbing to the next area and you'll see a block beside a see-saw and a ledge over a gap. Pick up the block and take it to the far end of the see-saw so the one end that's up is the one facing the ledge and the gap. Head to this end and grab the wooden plank off the top of this ledge. You'll notice there is a little lip on the bottom left hand side of this ledge, so slide the wooden plank under there to prevent it from tipping up and falling when you eventually jump on it. It may take a bit of trial and error, but keep sliding it forward and jumping on the end of it until you're able to jump on the plank and then jump across to the next area.

In this new area we're going to go for the final achievement. Walk forward and you'll fall into a mini-pit. Climb out and you'll see some swinging pendulum things. Wait until the first one swings back to the left, then quickly jump to the ledge and turn and face it with your arms up. When it comes, you'll grab it and your body will freak out. Once the pendulum reaches the pinnacle of it's swing on the right, let go and try and jump off the pendulum and you'll be launched to a ledge on the right. Head up some platforms here and face the way you came and you'll see some wooden hanging platforms. Line yourself up with the right side of the wooden platform and jump onto it and hang on. The platform will break and it will swing all the way to a green ledge. Once you're over it, let go and you'll unlock:

Clockwork
Pass the pendulum obstacle course in 60 seconds in “Aztec”
