Mission 1 to 5

Who's Your Daddy? is a reenactment
computer game made by Joe Williams.
The game financed $1,500 through the
crowdfunding stage Kickstarter and got
support on Steam Greenlight. An early
access adaptation of the game was made
accessible in late December 2015.

The game happens in a two-story home
with a few normal rooms. Players
frequently swap jobs each round.

 

1. Clean Toys

Objective: Put away the baby's toys

Instruction: Pick up the toys and place
them into the toybox.

The toys include: Small wooden blocks,
big colored blocks, the ball, and the toy
piano.

2. Clean the Pool

Objective: Remove foreign objects from
the pool

Instruction: Jump on in, and grab and
toss out the four balls.

3. Pick up the Poop

Objective: Throw away the kitchen
poops

Instruction: Select each poop and
drag them to the trash can, where
they will disappear on impact.

There are 14 poops hidden, all
within the kitchen.

They are located:
1. In the fruit bowl
2. In front of the fridge
3. In the freezer
Inside of the kitchen island
(the cabinet below the fruit
4. bowl)
The pull-out drawer to the
5. left of the stove
6. Inside of the stove
7. Inside of the microwave
On the kitchen counter, to
8. the right of the stove
On top of all of the cabinets,
9. in the corner
Inside the cabinet above the
10. stacked glasses
11. In the sink
12. In the cabinet below the sink
13. On a dining chair
In the corner behind the
dining table (by the sliding
14. glass door)

 

 

4. Put Dishes Away

 

Objective: Place the dishes in the kitchen
cupboards

Instruction: There are 5 plates on the
counter, 5 glasses on the counter, and 3
glasses in the sink.

One at a time, pick the dishes up and
place them into the black cabinets.

The glasses are delicate, so take short
steps as to not build up too much
momentum.

 

5. Wash the Car

Objective: Touch the sponge to the car

Instruction: The sponge will spawn
somewhere in the room. Find it and drag
it to the car!

The most common spawns are on top of
the work table, and in the stacks of tires
(behind the car).
