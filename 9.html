REDSTONE LIBRARY

MAP INFO

We have created a neoclassical library with a round book-lending-hall and two additional side wings equipped with reading rooms and a grand staircase.

The interiors also reflect the neoclassical style and were designed with a huge attention to detail.

The lending-machine supports 10 books that can be borrowed individually from one slot!

This mechanism was designed by us, without any templates.
