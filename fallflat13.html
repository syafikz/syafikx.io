Human Fall Flat Level 13 - Thermal

Level 13 - Thermal

Text Walkthrough:

Time for another title update level! Similar to Dark, this is quite a lengthy level, but aside from a handful of frustrating bits and one of the tougher achievements of the game, it's not too bad to get through. To find it from the level select screen, you'll have to select the Extra Dreams option.

As you start, turn the camera to the right to find a small cabin. Pull the door open to get inside and grab the black plank on your right hanging off the edge. You'll now want to get this plank back outside of the cabin which can be a bit of a task. You can either hold it above your head, horizontally as straight as you can, and try to get it out of the door and around to the right, or you can lay it horizontally and push it through the crack in the cabin wall opposite where you found the plank. Once you have it outside, take it with you around the side of the rocky outcropping (left of where you started) to find a big gap. You should know what to do here: either hold the plank above your head so you can get it to bridge the gap entirely and walk safely across, or push it along the ground so that it partly sticks out over the gap and jump off the end of it to reach and grab onto the other side.

Once you're safely over the gap, follow the ledge around and at the end, jump down to the left onto another bit of land. Follow this around to the left, then head right up a slope towards some pillars. If you fall in this next part, you'll land on ice and will have to slide back down to the bottom; careful not to slide off the edge entirely! Climb up the two pillars, then jump onto the hanging rope. Swing back and forth on this to get some momentum so you can safely jump off onto the land on the left. Climb up two ledges to your right to reach the top. From here, follow the path into the cave opening ahead, and around the corner to find another cabin. Enter and turn right to find a shelf up against the wall. Grab this and pull it to the right to reveal an opening in the wall behind it. Before moving on, make sure to grab the thin blue stick propping the window open and pull it out to close the window. It's easiest to do this if you grab the stick by the very bottom.

Once you're ready to move on, go through the other door to come back out into the cave. Follow the path along until you enter a room with some torches, crates, and an ice wall with an unlit fire next to it. Grab a crate and place it underneath one of the torches, then carefully climb atop it so you can grab the torch and pull it out of its holder. With the torch in hand, backtrack down the path to enter the cabin again. Place the black end of the torch into the furnace here to light it, then head back out and down the path into the room we just got the torch from. If you did not close the window before, the gust of wind blowing through it will snuff out your torch. Once you're back in the cave with the lit torch, place it onto the fire to light it and wait a moment for it to melt the ice wall and open the way forwards.

Back outside, jump onto the land on your left (sliding down the ice here will just drop you back outside of the cabin!) and follow it to the edge before dropping down onto another thin ledge below you. Head right along it and climb up onto the wooden platform, then head left through the opening into a big area with several structures. As you enter, turn immediately right and head up the ramp, then towards the edge to find a little wooden pillar with planks sticking out of it that can be turned. Push this all the way counter-clockwise to raise a hanging platform to your left. Jump onto this before jumping again off the end to grab a ledge connected to a red cabin with a blue door. Head inside and grab the hook that's laying on the floor, then take this back outside with you and drop off the ledge to the ground level. Right by where you land is a wooden staircase heading back up; head up it with the hook in hand. At the top, you'll reach a section of the staircase that has broken. Simply lay the hook over the gap here and jump over it yourself, then grab the hook to pull it to the other side.

From here, head straight forwards, back down the ramp, and up the small set of wooden stairs across from you. At the top of this, you'll see a raised wooden structure with a black bar attached to its underside that has a T-shape sticking out from it. You'll want to grab the hook from its bottom so you can lift it up and get it latched onto this T-shape. This can be a bit fiddly to do, but once you've got it latched, move backward to pull the bar with the hook and drop down a new set of wooden stairs. Grab the hook again and take it up these new stairs with you. Follow the wooden path around to the right, carefully jumping over the gaps on the way and keeping the hook held high so it doesn't get caught on the ground.

Down to your left, you'll see a new gated area which is where we want to get to, but we'll have to avoid entering through the gate to get our first achievement of the level. Once you reach another cabin, keep following the wooden ledge around to the other side of this to find a giant rope going over to the roof of the red cabin from earlier. Like in the Castle level, you'll want to grab the hook from the very bottom and lift it right the way up to get it caught onto the rope. Once it's definitely hooked on to the rope, jump off to slide down to the opposite roof; make sure to jump slightly to the opposite side of the rope from where your hook is caught so that the hook doesn't slip off the rope and drop you back down to the ground, as it can be frustrating to have to climb up again!

After you've landed safely on the roof, turn left to find a rocky pillar below with several ledges. You might want to drop the hook down to the ground (off the side facing where we just came from) as a fail-safe in case you mess up this next bit. Jump down to this pillar once you're ready, with your arms outstretched so that you'll definitely grab onto it, then clamber up the ledges to the top. Straight across from you is an open window going into another red cabin. Line yourself up with this window and jump over to it before climbing up through it. If you miss this jump, you're unfortunately going to have to take the hook back up and get over that rope again. Once you're safely inside, drop down the cabin to the bottom of the staircase and head through the blue door here. Once you're outside, having not entered through the main gate, move forward until you get a checkpoint and you'll unlock:

 

Hook, Line and Jumper

Enter the compound in "Thermal" without passing through the main gate

In this new area, head immediately right through the doorway to come out into a big open room with a giant drill. The first thing we'll do here is gather four coloured electrical cables for our next achievement, which can be a tough one. Down on the ground to the left of the drill, you'll see the first cable, a yellow one. Leave it there for now, walking past it to the opposite side of the room to climb up the ledge and end up by some train tracks, a green bridge that is propped up and a crate. Grab this crate and take it to the right, up a set of wooden stairs to find another red/purple cabin. Place the crate next to this cabin and climb up onto it to get up to the roof. Grab the green cable and drop it off the edge of the roof down to the ground.

Drop back down to where you placed the crate and head into the cabin to find another cable, this one blue. Grab it and take it to the middle of the room to drop it with the yellow cable. Climb back up and get the crate by the cabin, then head back down the path to the green bridge and push it so it falls down to the ground. Place the crate at the bottom of this bridge, in between it and the wall. You'll now want to grab the yellow and blue cables in each hand and jump off the bridge onto the crate so you can jump up onto the ledge and get the cables up there with you, which can be a bit fiddly to get right. You might find it easier if you just take one cable at a time.

Once you've got them up, you'll be next to a metal ramp that heads up to the top of the room. Take your yellow and blue cable up it until you end up in a building. Pull open the blue door on your left to enter a room with the last cable, a red one, and several coloured connectors. Drop the cables here and head back out into the main room to grab the green one. If you dropped it all the way down to the bottom, you'll have to jump off the crate with it again to get up onto that ledge. Once you have it in hand, take it up the ramp and drop it in the room with the other cables.

With all four of the cables together, we can now go for our second achievement. You'll need to connect all of the cables correctly to the colored ports here in under 35 seconds, with the timer starting as soon as you have connected the first one. This is no easy feat, so before starting, you should prepare a little bit by placing the cables close to their corresponding ports so you can connect them as quickly as possible once you're ready to go. When everything's set up, you can go ahead and start connecting them. I'd recommend going from left to right, so red/blue first, then green and yellow last. Make sure you're grabbing the ends of the cable so you can guide them into the ports as best you can. Once they're all properly connected, wait a moment and if you were successful in doing it fast enough, you'll unlock:

 

Amped!

Attach all electrical cables in 35 seconds or less in "Thermal"

Now if you didn't unlock it, not to fear as you thankfully don't need to restart from the checkpoint and gather all the cables again. You can simply disconnect all the cables entirely, then put them back in place near their ports to prepare for another attempt. I can confirm this as I attempted it three times without restarting from the checkpoint and successfully got it once the cables had been connected fast enough on my third try. This is one of the more frustrating achievements in the game without a doubt and definitely requires some luck that the physics work out for you, but keep at it and you will get it done soon enough!

Once you've got that achievement, you can head back out of the room and outside again into the main area where the drill will now be spinning as we have just powered the compound. Drop back down to the bottom level and head up the opposite ledge where the train tracks are and where we got the crate. Head right along the tracks to find a minecart with gold nuggets. Grab one of these and head back to the left along the tracks, past the green bridge to find an elevator that we can now use. Step onto it with the nugget in hand and press the left button to send the elevator up. At the top, step straight off onto a moving conveyor belt. To the left of it, you'll see a long glass window. Make sure you're moving around so the conveyor belt doesn't push you straight off the edge into the abyss, and swing the nugget so that it breaks the window. Climb on through into another room and head left to find some controls overlooking the drill. Pull back the red lever here and it will cause the drill to make a hole in the ground.

Head back out (you can jump back through the window or move the file cabinet blocking the door which leads out to the corridor from earlier) and drop down to the drill, then through the new hole underneath it. Turn around and follow the path along until the cave opens up. You'll notice these geysers in the ground periodically blowing out air. Walk along the right side of the cave to find a geyser with a rock blocking its hole. Pick up this rock, then turn around and place it in the geyser directly ahead. With that one now blocked, head back to the previously blocked geyser and its air will now launch you high enough to grab onto the ledge/platform above it to its left. Climb up the next ledge to end up by several pillars leading to another rock. Carefully jump along the pillars to the platform with the rock, then with this new rock in hand, jump back along the pillars. Take your time as the pillars will shift under your weight.

Once you're back in the main cave, drop down with the new rock and place it on the first geyser on the left again (the one that was originally blocked that we just used to get up here). With two geysers blocked, the remaining geyser will blow out an extra powerful blast of air, so head right to find that geyser and face the wall to its left to get blown up onto a ledge. Climb your way up another two ledges, then turn left and jump across the giant pillar to land on a seesaw-like contraption. Move back on it so that it tilts up as far as possible to the next ledge above, then run forwards and jump off at the end so you can clamber up onto that ledge. Drop straight down into a part of the cave with another geyser and a tall, thin pillar. Use the air from this geyser to reach the top of the pillar and pull down another rock atop it. Place the rock on top of the geyser here, then climb up through the hole in the wall on the right to drop back into the main cave again.

Back down there, grab the rock from the geyser nearest where you land (the one on its own, closest to where we originally entered the cave) and place it on the only unblocked geyser, which we just used to launch to the top of the cave. Now return back to the geyser on its own again, and facing away from the other two geysers, use the air to launch right up to an opening at the very top of the cave. It can take a few tries, but once you've successfully climbed up there, you're free of all this geyser business!

Once you're at the top, head straight forward, climbing up more ledges and over some rocks blocking your way. You'll emerge by a set of tracks, with a minecart to your left and a rope ahead of you that has a ring attached to it. Grab this rope and take it around to the other side of the cart, then place the ring so it hooks onto the back of the cart. Now grab the other end of the rope with both hands and walk down the track going outside, away from the big red door, pulling the cart with you until you drop off the end of the tracks and find yourself hanging over the edge. You'll see an icy ledge down below, up against the rock wall. Swing back and forth on the rope until you have enough momentum to let go and safely land on the ledge.

You now have a brief platforming section where you need to follow the wall to carefully jump along the ledges. You'll quickly reach a frozen waterfall and will have to climb onto it before jumping up to the next ledge, being cautious not to slip off. Head left from here and jump over to the next ledge to find some stalactites hanging from the ceiling. Grab the nearest one with one hand, then swing back and forth from it until you can grab the second stalactite with your other hand. Once you're safely on that one, grab it with your other hand and swing back and forth so you can jump off to the right to land by one last cabin.

You'll see the exit sign above the door of the cabin, but before we can finish up the level, there's one last achievement that can be easily missed right at the end here if you're not careful. You'll get a checkpoint as you approach the cabin, so you can restart from it if you mess up. Pull open the door and straight ahead of you inside, you'll see some gold bars through another doorway, but don't approach them yet. We want to finish the level with one of these bars in hand for the last miscellaneous achievement, but the floor in front of the bars will break as you step onto it, and the hole the breaking floor creates is the exit of the level, so you need to approach the bars properly in order to get over to them without falling through the hole and ending the level. If the floor breaks and you're on it, quickly pause and load the checkpoint before the level can end.

Ready to go? Move to either side of the doorway ahead, keeping well clear of the middle of the room, and grab onto the wall with one hand, then move sideways onto the floor in front of the doorway so that it breaks. Providing you're grabbed onto the wall, you shouldn't fall and can safely move back to the side again. Now that you can see where the hole is, it's a matter of getting over it to reach those gold bars. You can try and grab onto the side of the doorway and swing around to reach the remaining bits of floor in the next room, which is on either side, or my preferred method is to back up, angle myself so that I'm in line with the floor and then run before jumping over the hole to grab onto the floor. However you do it, again, be ready to quickly pause and load from the checkpoint if you fall. Once you're safely in the room, grab any one of the gold bars and drop down the exit hole with it in hand. The level will end, kicking you out to the main menu, and after a short while, you'll unlock:

 

Wrapped Up

Complete "Thermal"
