Human Fall Flat Level 14 - Factory

Level 14 - Factory

Text Walkthrough:

Another update level to get through! Like Thermal, you can find it in the Extra Dreams section on the level select screen. This might appear to be quite a complex level at first, but it's fairly linear and straightforward once you get into it. In terms of achievements, there's one for not turning the lights on in the first half of the level which requires you to avoid touching any of the five light switches; the only real effect of this is it will be harder to see in some areas and you may need to bump your brightness up. I'm not sure whether getting too close to the light switches will cause the achievement to glitch like with the wooden bars achievement in Dark, but it's best to keep away from them when I mention them to be safe and only press switches that I specifically tell you to press.

You will also have to find four radios and throw them out of the the level (as in off the edge into the void) for another achievement, the locations of which will be pointed out clearly as we go through the level. I did however have this achievement glitch on me on my first attempt for some reason, and I suspect it might be to do with either reloading checkpoints or sitting on the Home page whilst still playing the game, so try to avoid doing either of those things to be safe.

As you start the level, head into the building immediately in front of you. The red switch in the doorway is the first light switch of the level, so leave it alone so as not to void that achievement and turn your brightness up in here if need be. Inside the room, press the red switch on the brown box to the right to drop a bar from the chute next to it. Directly opposite that chute on the other side of the room are two receptacles with red lights next to them; lift the bar up to get it inside one of them so the red light turns green, then get another bar from the chute and place that inside the other receptacle. Once both are in place, press the red switch on the other brown box to the left. The bars will get turned into a gear that drops out from another chute.

Turn around from there to find some machinery, but before placing the gear in the obvious spot, look atop the machinery to find your first Radio 1/4. To get it, place the gear in the machinery, then quickly turn left and grab onto the right side of metal sliding door as it goes up. Once it's reached the top, you can swing from there to land on the machinery and grab the radio. If you fail to do this, it's also possible with some difficulty to climb onto a small black pipe on the right side of the machinery, then up to the radio from there.

Head down the newly opened hallway with the first radio in hand. Avoid the first red switch on the right as you round the corner because it's the second light switch. Just head right past it, then make a U-turn to the left in the next big room to find an opening going back outside. You can throw the radio off the edge here (Radio 1/4 Thrown). Ignore the red switch out here too as it's also a light switch. Now time for some platforming. Next to where we are outside is a giant spinning wheel. Facing away from the building we just came out of, grab onto the wheel so you are spinning with it, then release after a spin or two to launch further into the level onto a brown platform with another wheel. Use this wheel in the same way to launch to the next platform. This one has some stairs leading up to a spinning ball. Seeing the pattern here? Grab onto the ball and launch once again to another platform with a second ball. This last time, you'll be aiming to fly into the giant golden tube that looks like a horn; once you've landed in it, follow the linear path to emerge by a staircase that leads up to a red button. Stand on this and it will lower a bridge to the previous area.

With that done, head left from the button to find three levers and a red switch overlooking a giant claw grabber with a laser coming from it. Our aim here is to use the grabber to pick up the golden ball to the right of it and take it back over to the left to drop it into the fire next to you. As you'd expect, the levers control the grabber, with the left lever moving it backwards and forwards, the middle lever moving it left and right and the right lever moving it up and down. It's a bit confusing, but you'll get the hang of it. Get the grabber over to the right towards the golden ball, and when the laser shows that it's directly above the ball, pull the right lever back until the grabber is all the way down, then push the switch to pick the ball up.

Now push the right lever so the grabber goes back up, then manoeuvre it over to the fire; careful with your movements as the ball can fall out if you jerk the grabber about too much. Use the laser as a guide and once the ball is above the fire, press the red switch again to release it. Once it's in, a little receptacle with a golden sphere will rise up to the left; grab this and head back the way we came, back over the bridge to the left and into the factory again. On the left wall in here, you'll see more receptacles with red switches and the second of these will have a sphere symbol on it. Drop the sphere in here and you'll open up some more doors on the other side of the room.

Let's head through the left one first leading out to a conveyor belt. The switch out here is another light switch to be ignored. We now have an obstacle course of sorts to contend with, beginning with some machinery that stamps down onto the belt. Jump past one when it's up and wait until it's safe to pass the next. After that will be some pushers to jump past again, followed by another stamper. Next up are some spinning bars which are simple enough to deal with. Step on the red button past those to lower a bridge back to the start in case you fall in this next section. Climb up a conveyor belt to reach another obstacle, some giant swings. As soon as one has passed, quickly jump along the belt and you should make it through.

On the other side are some hanging... let's call them strings to swing along. Jump to the first and grab onto it, then release one of your hands and swing towards the next wire so you can grab onto it with your free hand. Release your hand from the first wire and repeat this process until you're across the gap. Your final obstacle are some swings that slam together; you'll need to time a running start here so you can jump through as soon as the swings part and make it past in time; don't worry too much if they slam on you though as they won't kill you. Past those, sitting on a white square, is a piece of machinery in the shape of a lever. Grab this and drop into the opening behind it to end up back by the factory. Head back in; the first receptacle on the right will have the switch shape on it. Drop the piece in there so the light goes green like before.

Now go through the other door that opened up to the right of the previous one. Out here is your final light switch, so ignore it as usual and continue down the path. Pull the red switch you come to all the way down until it releases sparks and you'll cause all the gears and machinery in the area to start operating. Time for some more platforming. Hop along the first few gears and you'll quickly come to more of those hanging strings from the previous area. This time, they're moving in a circle, so grab onto one of them and let it take you all the way around until you reach a brown platform. Step onto it and jump onto the next brown platform when it lowers down to you. Ride it back up and jump onto the top of another gear, then head onto the smaller gear to the left. From here, turn right so you're facing the side of a big gear, then jump over and latch onto it so it takes you further up. At the top, hop your way along the gear and over the top of some more, then jump down at the end of the makeshift path to a bridge below with a red button. Stepping onto this, much like before, will lower a staircase back down to the start.

We're not done with the platforming yet though, as if you turn around from the red button, you'll find your next gear to ride. Grab onto the sides of this to head straight up into another gear; ride this too to find a small gear to step onto at the top. Carefully drop off the edge of it onto the next gear below, then ride the moving brown platform when it comes to you to clear the gap to the next gear. Jump off this one to the next set of gears to the right, and jump from those onto one of the spinning brown platforms. Lastly, when you're close enough, jump out and grab onto the hanging yellow ball, then swing back and forth to build up some momentum so you can drop down to the platform below. Here is your last machinery piece in the shape of a base; pick it up, then head into the opening by it and follow the tube until you drop back by the factory again. You're going to want to drop this piece in the furthest receptacle on the left wall. As usual, you'll know it's the right one when the red light turns green.

Now it's time to use the machine pieces we've collected to create a switch that will open up access to the next area, which is a rather tedious and confusing process, so apologies if the instructions here are a bit hard to follow. To begin, we'll need to get a bar, so head to the furthest red switch on the right wall, by the opening to the first area we went in. Press this red switch to release a bar, then place that in the next receptacle to the right with a bar symbol on it, which is by the one we placed the sphere in. Press the red switch next to that receptacle to eventually drop a completed sphere from the chute next to it. Try to grab it as soon as it falls, because it is a real pain to see it if it drops on the floor thanks to the lights being off. In the opposite corner of the room, next to the opening of the last area we went in, are three receptacles. The furthest one on the left has the symbol of the completed sphere on it, so drop your new sphere in there to turn the light green again. We'll be placing the completed machine pieces in these three receptacles.

We now just need to repeat this process for the next two pieces, so grab two more bars from the far machine and place them in the receptacle next to the lever piece/base piece, then press the red switch to the left of them to complete each piece and grab them as they fall to place them in their appropriate receptacles by where we put the completed sphere. Once all three completed pieces are in place in the three receptacles, push the final red switch to the left of them to finally form a completed switch. Put this in the red outline of a switch sitting nearby in the middle of the room, then once it's in place, pull it to lower the floor and reveal the opening to the next area. Drop down into there and as you enter a room with a giant blue portal (don't go in it yet!), providing you did not touch any of the light switches, you'll unlock:

 

After Hours

Now before entering the portal, check under the small set of stairs leading up to it to find the second Radio 2/4. We'll need to get this thing back outside so we can throw it out of the level, so head back out of the room with it in hand. Getting the radio back up the ledges that we dropped down can be a pain though; what I found works best is to grab the radio with one hand, then hold it high above your head and use the other hand to pull yourself up onto the ledge. It can be a bit annoying to do this without accidentally grabbing onto the radio or having it push you back down, but you'll get up there with it eventually. Do the same again with the next ledge, then take it with you through any of the openings leading outside and throw the damn thing off the level (Radio 2/4 Thrown). Good riddance to that irritating music! Now head back inside, drop down again and head through the portal to the next half of the level.

You'll teleport into a new indoors section with a minor puzzle. Ahead, you'll see a machine with giant boxes that have symbols on them and behind you is a golden lever that you would normally pull to reveal the correct combination for the symbols, but I'm pretty sure this is not necessary, especially because the combination appears not to be randomized and is the same in every video I can find. Ignore that switch then and just make your way across the spinning platforms to reach the symbol machine. Take your time doing this as it can be a pain to get across with how fast the platforms spin. Once you have that combination, pull the lever on the machine to open the door to the next area. If it isn't working and the lever has a red light under it, make sure the boxes are aligned as straight and correctly as possible without being halfway between symbols as this can prevent the door from opening. With it open, head on through to the second room. As you enter, you'll immediately see another Radio 3/4 up on a platform to the right. To reach it, you'll need to wait for the giant arm of the machine here to swing over to where you are and stamp down onto the ground. You'll quickly see what I mean. Once it's down, grab onto it and as it swings over to the right, release with the right timing to fling over onto the ledge with the radio. It can take a few tries to get this, but once you're up there with the radio, simply grab it and throw it off the edge, making sure it falls out of the level completely (Radio 3/4 Thrown). Now to continue, grab onto the arm again when it's by you and hold onto it as it takes you further into the room, then let go when it stamps back down onto the ground. Use another arm over here in the same way to make it into the final room of the level.

That's right, this is the final room before we finish, but there's quite a bit to do in here. Spread around the room are four different types of items, and we'll have to throw one of each into the giant fire in the middle for another achievement. This however does NOT include Radio 4/4 which is high up on the right and has to be thrown off the level like usual when we get to it. So, to begin, in the doorway at the start of the room is a button with a blue outline around it. Press this button to lower a nearby barrier with another button behind it. Pressing that one will drop down a bright yellow box. These things can only be moved for a few seconds before they turn blue and become completely stuck in place, so lift it up high above your head and carry it over to the left wall, placing it in between the short platform that looks like a chair/stepladder and the tall platform to the right of it. If you mess up with these boxes, just press its button again to spawn another one. Once you've got it against the wall, keep it held there until it turns blue and sticks in place, then climb up the chair/stepladder and use the box as a step to reach the tall platform and press the next button with a blue outline up there.

This will lower another nearby barrier that also has a button that will drop down a yellow box. Press that button, but take this new box over to the LEFT of the chair/stepladder this time (just left of where we put the other box), placing it against the wall there so you can reach the top of the platform to the left. Up there are several gold bars (Item 1/4); throw them all down to the ground. If you look across the room from here, past the second blue button we pressed, you'll see our next item high up on another platform. Drop back down and spawn the second box back in with the further red button, then hold it against the left wall again, this time to the right of the platform with the button. You can then climb back up, make your way to the button again and then get over to that platform with what looks like giant bullets or lamps (Item 2/4). Throw both of those down to the ground too.

Now to deal with the right side of the room. Before we place any boxes, head over to that side and grab the golden wheels (Item 3/4) off the lower part of the middle platform, just getting those thrown onto the ground again. With that done, drop either of the boxes down and quickly place it in the back-right corner, against the wall between another chair/stepladder and a taller platform. Once it's in place, get the other box and put it against the right wall between that platform and the next one. You now have a path again to make it to the next button with a blue outline, so clamber up the other chair/stepladder and hop along the boxes to push that button which will allow us to get the final box

From here, you'll be able to see the final item and the radio together on the last platform, so drop back down, spawn the last box in and place it further along the wall again so you can jump across all three boxes and climb up onto the last platform. Up here is a gear (Item 4/4) and that last radio; throw the gear onto the ground, then grab the radio and drop down with it. Head back out of the room and throw it off the level for good. Providing you threw all of the other radios out of the level as well, you'll finally unlock:

 

Radio Silence

As mentioned before, this achievement glitched for me and did not unlock on my first attempt even though I had definitely thrown all the radios out of the level, forcing me to play the level over a second time. I did restart from checkpoints quite a bit whilst writing this walkthrough, and I also sat on the Home page for quite a while outside of the game, which I suspect might have caused it to glitch in some way, so avoid doing either of those things and the achievement should hopefully unlock for you without a problem.

Now you should have thrown all four types of items in that final room down to the floor now, so get to work on throwing one of each of these things into the fire: a gold bar, a golden wheel, a bullet/lamp and a gear. Getting them in there can a pain, but the best way is to try and lift them up to get them resting on the edge of the fire, then let go. If it doesn't fall in on its own, you should be able to push it in from there with one of your hands. Once you've thrown at least one of all the items in, you'll unlock:

 

How to get Fired!

Throw one of each item in the final room into the fire in “Factory”

The achievement actually unlocked for me before I'd even managed to throw my last item in, so it seems to be a bit glitchy in your favour at least. In the event that it doesn't unlock, try and throw in more of the items to see if that does the trick. To finish the level, find three black squares on the ground on the opposite side of the room from where you entered. You simply have to get all three of the boxes onto these squares, so spawn them in again and get them all in place. Once you've done this, the walls of the room will lower to reveal a massive blue portal. Step onto the small platform in front of it next to the exit sign and wait as you get lifted up to a bridge. Follow it into the portal and as the level ends, kicking you back to the main menu, you'll unlock:
