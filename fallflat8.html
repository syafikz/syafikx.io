10. Human Fall Flat Level 8 - Power Plant

Level 8 - Power Plant

Text Walkthrough:

We now begin the second last level of the game (yay!) but the last with any miscellaneous achievements (boo!), Power Plant. More good news though, this is the level where I unlocked the achievement for jumping 1000 times. Odds are you'll get it this level, but if not we still have one more level to go and another one to replay, so it will come soon.

 

Trampoline

Jump 1000 times

 
1 guide

 

Now you'll start in an area with a fan, plugged in to a battery in the wall, connected by a red and a blue cable. Take the red cable out of both sockets and the blue cable out of the socket beside the fan. Take the other end of the blue cable and connect it to the red socket beside where the other end of the blue cable is. If done correctly, you'll hear a loud bang and get thrown across the room, and you'll also unlock:

 

Electricity 101

Short-circuit the wires in "Power Plant"

 
1 guide

 

Now take both red and blue cables and walk through the fan into the next area. here you'll see two outlets, one by a door and one on a wall. Connect the blue wire to the blue sockets and the red wire to the red sockets to open the door to the next area. Here you'll see an outlet next to a door and a battery in the distance. Grab the battery and bring it by the door. Plug the red wire into the red socket on the battery and outlet by the door, and do the same for the blue wire. If done correctly, this will open the gate. Now we will begin the long and tedious set up for the achievement Will it Fry? which is powering an appliance with three batteries. Once the gate is open, disconnect the battery and bring it through with you. Now off in the corner to your left, you'll see a fan that's knocked down as a result of your electricity training earlier. Head over and drag it onto the button to open the door to the next area, and make sure you grab the battery and bring it with you. Once you head through the door, turn around and on the wall immediately to the left of the door is a switch. Flip the switch and the conveyor belt to your left will start moving. Jump and grab it and ride it around until you're on top of it. Once you're on top you'll see a platform with a box. Jump up onto it, grab the box, then jump back down to the platform and run across it back to where you started. Place the box on the button and head through the door to the next area, but don't forget your battery!

In this next area, drop the battery you came in with close to the door you just came through. Immediately to your right against the wall you'll see a charger and in the distance, another battery and some cables nearby. Drag the charger to the battery in the distance and plug the cables into the charger and the battery to charge it up as it's dead. Once it's charged, there will be a green light on the charger, so disconnect the battery and the cables and plug the cables into the battery and the outlet beside the gate. Once they're plugged in, push the lever beside the gate up and it should open. Make sure you open it all the way, then walk through the gate and you'll see some long cables and a generator. Plug the cables into the generator and the outlet beside the gate in front of you thats closed. Now walk around to the back of the generator and you'll see a red pully thing. Pull this three times and the generator will start, opening the gate to the next area.

In here, you'll see a forklift and another gate. You need to drive the forklift back into the previous area, past the generator and to the other gate where the charger is up against. The controls are straightforward on the forklift: the wheel turns it, push the lever on the right forward to go forward and pull back to go backwards. It's super tough to maneuver so watch the video guide if you're struggling. Once you manage to get the forklift to the gate, line it up with the gate and drive into it so the forks are underneath the gate. Now it used to be possible to raise the forklift arms to push the gate up high enough to place the charger under it and keep it held up, and this is what you will see in the video walkthrough. However, this has now been patched since the addition of the new DLC levels and the forklift will not be able to lift the gate any higher than it already is. You can easily work around this by just using the forklift itself to hold up the gate though which still works without a problem. So hop off and use the lever on the left of the wheel to raise the arms of the forklift, and do this until they're right underneath the gate. Now carry the two batteries through to the other side of the gate. You need to make absolutely sure both batteries are on this side as the gate will close when you move the forklift away from it. Now hop back onto the forklift and drive it back to the area you first got it. Once here, you'll see a gate and you need to do the same thing here that you did with the previous gate. So drive up and get the forks underneath it, then hop off and lift the arms up to raise it and keep it there. Now backtrack back to where you left your two batteries and carry them all the way into the new section the forklift just opened up for you, as well as the two long cables that were hooked up to the generator.

This next area is directly across a gap from where you rode the conveyor belt to get the box down. Here you'll see a statue holding a battery. This battery is important for two achievements, so we have to climb up there and get it. Stand facing the front of the statue and you'll see the pedestal it's on has some indents in it. Use the cheese method to get up here and stand on the pedestal with it. If you can't get the cheese method to work, you can stack your two batteries on top of one another against the wall, then climb on top of the stack and onto the wall from there. This is easier said than done, and if you're really struggling to get the second battery on top of the other, you can place one battery against the ramp by the gate you used to enter this area, then head up the ramp with the other battery and place it on top of the first battery, then carefully pull the stack into place against the statue base. Once you're up, stand on the right side of the pedestal and you'll notice the tablet the statue is holding is tilted. So jump towards it and climb up onto it. Careful as it's tough to maneuver on here. You not need to jump up to his hat. So from the tablet, jump and grab onto his hat and pull yourself up so you're standing on his head. Once here you'll see the battery is almost within reach, so hold out your arms and jump towards it. You should grab onto the battery and fall down with it, unlocking:

 

Thief

Steal the battery from the statue in "Power Plant"

 
2 guides

 

If you're struggling with the cheese method, check out the video walkthrough to see how to do it. Now that we have the three batteries we need, we have to open something with them. Past the statue you'll see a gate with an outlet beside it, as well as some shorter cables nearby. Grab your three batteries and line them up in a row beside the outlet by the gate, and bring the set of long cords and the set of short cords.

Now it is important you do this EXACTLY as I'm going to tell you, otherwise you may need to do this setup again. In the video walkthrough I did it out of order and the achievement didn't pop. Next time I did it though, it unlocked for me. You may even do it perfectly and it still might not unlock according to some people, so hopefully all your hard work doesn't go to waste, because it's annoying having to set this up.

First, I'm going to label the three batteries you have lined up beside the outlet. There's the left battery, middle battery, and right battery. First thing you want to do is take the short blue cord and plug one end into the blue socket of the outlet by the gate, then plug the other end into the blue socket on the right battery. Next, take a short red cord and plug one end into the red socket on the right battery, then plug the other end into the blue socket on the middle battery. Next, take the long blue cord and plug one end into the red socket on the middle battery, then plug the other end into the blue socket on the left battery. Finally, take the long red cord and plug one end into the red socket on the left battery, then plug the other end into the red socket on the outlet by the gate.

If done correctly, this should open the gate and you'll unlock:

 

Will it fry?

Feed an appliance with power from 3 batteries in "Power Plant"

 
5 guides

 

Make sure to consult the video guide if it will help you more. Don't forget you may have done it right and it might just not unlock. If that's the case you'll have to replay the level like I did.

Now that all that fun stuff is out of the way, we can continue on with the level. Head through the now open gate into the next area and you'll see three boilers on you right and some coal laying around. Jump up to the doors on the boilers and grab them to pull them down. The one on the right is lit but the other two are not, so you have to light them. Past the boilers turn to the right and you'll see a path leading underground in between a wall and a smoke stack. Head down here and you'll see a torch. Pick up the torch and head back up to the two boilers that are turned off. Stick the torch inside them and they should turn on. Now the problem is you don't have enough coal, so you have to go get some from the island across the bridge.

How are you going to do that? Well see that fancy dump truck in front of you? You get to drive that bad boy up the road in front of you to the quarry. Now climb up to the cab of the truck and you'll see a steering wheel and a lever to the right of it. What I did, to ensure the truck doesn't go flying off the road, was push the lever forward slightly. The truck has two forward and reverse speeds, one for a light tilt and one for a full tilt. I went with a slower speed just because I wasn't confident in my abilities to drive a truck at full speed. So take your time and work your way up the road in front of you. Refer to the video guide if you want some more help. Once you're at the quarry you'll see a machine with a shoot coming out of it at the bottom. You want to park the bed of the truck right under the shoot, then jump down and head into the cave on your right. Follow it around and you'll reach a massive pile of coal beside a conveyor belt. Now we are going to load up on coal for one of the miscellaneous achievements. Grab 10 pieces of coal and throw them onto the conveyor belt (I grabbed 2-3 extra just in case some got lost in transport). Once you've done this, head back to your truck and stand in the cab and wait until all the pieces have fallen into the bed of the truck. Once they've all fallen in, drive your truck straight and follow it around the cave and you'll reach another road that will take you back to where you started. Again take it slow down this hill, it's actually a bit easier to traverse because it's straighter and downhill, so you gather some more speed this way. Once you reach the main part of the level again, if you loaded up 10 pieces of coal from the quarry and successfully transported them back, you'll unlock:

 

Delivery boy

Deliver 10 pieces of coal to the main island in "Power Plant"

 
1 guide

 

Now drive the truck back to the boilers and hop off. On the left side of the truck should be a lever on ground level. Flip it and it will raise and lower the bed of the truck, so dump out all your coal. Now I'd suggest to check your achievement progress for the Petrolhead achievement, which requires you to travel 1 km with gas vehicles. You can just keep driving the dump truck or forklift around, whichever is easier for you, until you unlock it:

 

Petrolhead

Use any of the ground vehicles to travel 1km

 
1 guide

 

Now that we have our coal, we need to put three pieces into each of the boilers. Do this and smoke will start to rise from the smokestacks close to you. Head back to the smokestack that is beside the underground area where you got the torch and walk in. If the boilers are fully heated, you should get blown by the fan to the top of the smokestack. Once you're at the top, grab on and pull yourself up. Now, facing the next smokestack, jump over to it. There is a fan in here so just simply fall into the fan and it will keep you level with the top of the smokestack, making it easier to traverse to the other side. Once on the other side of the smokestack, pull yourself up and you'll see the last smokestack you need to jump to with the exit sign on it. You'll see a ledge just below the exit markings on this smokestack, so jump and grab for this ledge and pull yourself up. Now carefully edge your way to the left along this ledge and climb through the hole in the smokestack. While you're falling you'll unlock:

 

The End?
