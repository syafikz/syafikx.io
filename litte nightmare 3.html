The Hospital

 

After crawling through the window from outside, you’ll end up in the Hospital. Continue moving through the hallway here and underneath the bed ahead of you.

Sneak past the gated area until you reach the double doors at the end of the hallway. With Six’s help, push them open, but there’s nothing on the other side. Thankfully, Six will grab a hold of you so you don’t fall.

From here, jump to the beds that are hanging and start climbing up. Once you enter the next building, you’ll notice a machine immediately when you come in with a light above it. Take out the white fuse in it, which will close the door behind you.

Then, take this fuse over to the far right end of the hallway where there’s another glowing light. Put in the fuse and the door will open. This will bring you into an elevator shaft. However, once you land on it, it’ll start to drop.

Once it lowers a little, a vent will appear on the right. Hurry over to it and lift it open before the elevator falls, then crawl inside.

 

In this next room, you’ll find a flashlight on the ground. Toggle it on while navigating this next area, as the room is very dark. Continue moving forward into the next area.

At the back of this room with the beds, you’ll find glitching remains. Then, backtrack and go through the door on the right.

The end of this hallway is blocked, so head down the other hallway towards the door at the far right with light coming out of it. You’ll have to push a button here to open the door in this room, so head to the vending machine and pull the lever to get something to throw.

Pick up the can and throw it at the button to open the door. This will lead you into a room with another TV. Go up to it and interact with it, tuning it until you’re brought back into the long hallway.

Once it shuts off, and you’re back on your feet, go right all the way before heading upstairs. There’s an elevator on the second level and a gated area that requires fuses to work. There’s also a door that requires a key on the right, which you’ll find the answer to on the first level.

Go past the elevator into the X-ray room. You’ll notice by the scans on the wall that a key is hidden in a stuffed animal, which is in the next room. In this playroom, you'll also find a collectible hat on top of the bookshelf to your right.

Tip: You can also use the X-ray for fun. If you go behind it, Six will pull the lever then come join Mono so you can see their skeletons.

Unfortunately, even though it’s hidden in the stuffed bear in the photo, it’s actually in a different stuffed animal now. You’ll have to check out each one in the X-ray, but you’ll eventually find it in the

bunny.

Once you have the one with the key, grab it and head into the elevator right outside this room. This will take you down to an incinerator. So exit the elevator and head right. Push the stuffed animal in the hole, then pull the lever.

Once it’s done, hop back in and grab the key. Then, take the elevator back up and head up to the room with the locked door on the second level to the right of the staircase. Open it up and head inside.

Continue moving through this very creepy room and through the door on the other side. This will lead you into a hallway with several different prosthetic body parts strewn about. If you head to the back of this area, you’ll also find glitching remains. Then, turn around and head all the way down the hallway, past the door you came in.

You’ll reach a door with an open window. Six will boost you through it, and now you have to find a way to bring her through. Move through this room until you reach a box blocking your way at the end. Push it to the side and crawl underneath the table.

The hand will wiggle off of the body on the operating table, so keep an eye out for it. Climb over the metal shelf in front of you, then drop down. The living hand will appear and try to get you, so run to the table on the other side and climb up.

It’ll go underneath to follow you to the other side, so when you have an opening, run to the other side and start climbing up. It’ll follow you, so keep moving quickly through the vent. Once you drop down, head down the hallway and through the door on the left.

You’ll hear the living hand moving around above you, so start sprinting forward. It’ll pop down in front of you, so move around it to reach the table and climb up. These areas are safe, as it can’t reach this high up.

Then, climb up the shelves and drop down into this next area. Again, be prepared to run, as the hand is lurking close by. Climb up on the table in the middle of the room. Here, you’ll find the fuse!

Before grabbing it, though, you’ll have to take care of the hand. There’s a hammer on the floor, thankfully, so pick it up and smack it once it comes close. It’ll take three swings to fully take it down, but once you have it, you’re in the clear to take the fuse and go left.

There’ll be a box blocking this door, so move it out of the way. Then, take your hammer to smash open a hole in it to get through. Continue through to the next room, where you’ll find Six playing with some of the prosthetic limbs on the floor.

Once you’re back in the main room, head to the room on the far left side of the second level. You need to find one more fuse before you can get to the elevator.

Head towards the double doors in the back. To the left, you’ll find a gated area that Six can boost you through.

On the other side, you’ll find a switch you can pull, along with a headless body on the opposite side. This is a Patient. Be careful of it, though, as it’ll start to move the second the lights go out.

The thing about Patients is that they don't move when light is shining on them. So, for getting around this one, shine the light on it enough to get around it then run through the gate.

Once you’re past the Patient, head down the hallway until you see a gated area ahead of you with a couple more of them. Move down there and head through the door on your left.

Here, you’ll find several more Patients, and a couple will start to move towards you, so be ready with your flashlight. There’ll be a table on the left for you to slide under, and once you’re through there, continue forward.

Be prepared for more to come towards you, but make your way towards the door that’s down a little ways on the left and jump through it. On the other side, continue left and jump on the metal table to reach the vent. Then, climb through.

Once you climb out, you’ll land on a bed. Head into the hallway and back towards the gated area first. There's a hole on the bottom right you can go through. Then, go through the door on the left to find glitching remains. Once you've done that, head back out and down the hallway.

You’ll have to run through this next area, as Patient’s hands will push through the holes in the doors to try and grab you. You’ll have to zig-zag through here, but be careful so as not to get too close.

Take a right at the end of the hall and keep running. There’ll be a couple Patients lying on the floor that will try to get you, but Mono will automatically shine the flashlight on them while running. Then, quickly climb up the file cabinet at the end of the hallway and climb through the vent.

On the other side of this area, you’ll find a switch to turn some of the lights back on. Not all of them, but the ones in this area. So switch them back on, and head to the back of the room first. Move the box in the back left to reveal a vent. Crawl through and you'll find glitching remains by a bath tub. Then, head back into the shower room. On the right, there's a button you’ll have to push to get through, so throw one of the soap bars on the floor at it.

Here, you’ll find a Patient in a tub and one in a wheelchair. Move around them both to the other side. You’ll need to find a way to get through the next door, and unfortunately, it’ll require getting that Patient out of its wheelchair.

You’ll need to go back and flip the switch again to shut off the lights and get the Patient out of the wheelchair. So run back and flip it again to turn the lights off. Then, head towards the Patient a little ways until it gets up and starts to follow you. Use your flashlight to slow him down a bit, too.

Run back and flip the switch again to stop it in its tracks. Once the Patient has stopped moving, grab the wheelchair and push it over to the door. Then, climb on top of it and through to the next area.

Unfortunately, you’re back in the dark again. So, in this next area, you have to move through the Patients to get to the other side, and you’ll have many coming at you all at once.

Tip: If you want, you can navigate this room by sprinting straight through. Or, you can slowly move through by shining your light on them. If you choose the latter, try and gather all of the Patients in one area so you can cover them all with your light. This works a lot easier than spinning around and trying to cover them all at once.

Slide underneath the boards blocking the door at the end of this area and into the next room. Then, head to the switch on the wall and pull it to get your next fuse. There’ll be a slot on the gated door to your right where you can throw it over to Six. Then, she’ll put it in the proper slot and you can get out.

Make sure to grab it again on your way out. Now, put both fuses into the slots by the elevator. Six will grab the other one for you. Then, pull the switch on the elevator and head down.

This will lead you into an even darker area. Once you’re out of the elevator, head down a ways, then right. You’ll find a door with some cloth hanging out of it that you can climb up to get through.

On the other side, you’ll have another living hand come at you and one more trying to break out behind you. So grab the pipe on the ground to fight back. Six will hold the second one back for a little bit while you handle the first one, but it’ll eventually jump out.

Once both are down, head through the hole in the door that Six pulled the planks off of. Keep moving through this next room. You’ll hear creaking up against metal shelving. So head under the shelving ahead of you, and this is where you’ll meet the Doctor, who’s crawling on the ceiling.

Climb over the shelving into the next area and hide behind the boxes on the floor. When he’s busy and not looking, move to the next box. Take your time through this area, as one small noise will immediately get his attention.

Move through the vent on the side and stay under the beds. If he hears you, he will lift them up and search for you, so tread lightly. In order to open the door on the other side, you’ll need something to throw at the button. So keep hiding under the beds and move to the back of the room to find a block or toy to throw.

Once you hit the button, quickly go back and hide under the table to your left. The Doctor will crawl through and look around, but once he moves into the next area, you’re in the clear to head through. Make your way to the cabinets on the other side and climb up to the vent.

Inside the vent, you’ll have to move around some open areas, as The Doctor’s hands will pop up every now and then. Once you exit the other side, make your way across the ceiling to the hole on the end with a piece of clothing attached. Then, drop down into this new area.

You’ll need a key to open this next door, so head left. You’ll see one of the doors in the morgue slightly open, and next to it is a tray that can pull out. Pull it out just enough to hop inside, then Six will push you through.

On the other side, hop out and head left. The key is on a table to your left. So, in order to get it, you need to pull out the first tray on the bottom. Then, jump to the tray above it and pull that one out. Now, you need to reach that second tray, which requires jumping from the table.

Head back to the area you came from and pull out the first tray on the bottom right. From here, jump to the tray already out, then to the metal table, then to the larger table. Once you’re here, jump to the tray across from you and climb to the top, over to the table, and grab the key.

Before heading back to Six, you can find a collectible hat behind the door on the bottom left by the table.

Now, head back to where you came in from, hop in, and Six will pull you back. Unlock the door and head through. Once you’re in the next room, climb the filing cabinets behind you and jump from shelf to shelf until you reach the next vent.

Follow the vent up and you’ll enter a room that requires another fuse to move forward. So, head through the vent on the left into the next room, where the Doctor is at work once again. You'll have to find a way to get him out of here, as the fuse you need is on the shelf behind him.

Move underneath the table and, when he’s not looking, move to the next one. There’s another hole in the wall here that Six can boost you through. Once you’re in the next room, you’ll notice one of the Doctor’s Patients lying in a bed. So, to avoid waking him up, quietly move to the table ahead of you and climb up to the ceiling.

Up here, head left and you’ll notice a switch you can drop to on the far left side. Drop down and grab it to switch it off. Then, quickly hide under the Patient’s bed. This will trigger them to flatline, which will bring the Doctor in to operate.

While he’s busy, sneak out the door and back to Six. Now, grab the fuse in the back. Six will pull out one of the trays that you can hop on, then jump to the next one and up to the fuse. Once you’ve got it, head back to the door and put the fuse in to open it.

Unfortunately, this will trigger a chase scene with the Doctor, so get ready to run. As you make your way through the Hospital’s halls, Six will lead you to an incinerator. Lure the Doctor inside and crawl your way out below him. Once you’re out, close the door and say goodbye.

Now, it’s time to leave, so head for the switch by the elevator and pull it to exit. This will take you up to a different level of The Hospital where you can exit through a window.
