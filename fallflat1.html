Human Fall Flat Level 1 - Mansion

Level 1 - Mansion

Just a reminder that there is an achievement for completing the game in one sitting including the first four update / DLC levels (up to Ice, as all levels after that are considered separate from the main game and do not count). This means that you cannot exit the game at any point whilst going for the achievement. Reloading from checkpoints and restarting levels via the pause menu shouldn't void the achievement, but it might be best to avoid doing this to be on the safe side, so if you mess up any achievements and have to reload a checkpoint to try for it again, you can just keep on going and replay the level later to get the achievement(s). Keep all of this in mind if you wish to do everything in one playthrough.

Text Walkthrough:

We start off at the first level, Mansion. Right away we can earn two achievements my either rubber banding your controller or simply holding it for a bit in one spot. Position your camera so you're facing out into the great beyond (aka the edge of the map), and walk off. You'll fall down and fall right back onto the map again, unlocking your first achievement:

 

No escape

Fall and respawn once

 
1 guide

 

Now you can rubber band your controller for a few minutes while facing the edge of the world and allow your character to walk off 99 more times, unlocking:

 

Let it rain

Respawn 100 times

 
2 guides

 

With those two easy achievements out of the way, we can actually start playing the game. You can pick up the yellow walkie talkie for a tutorial but if you read and memorized the hints and tips you won't need it. Walk up the stairs and push open the doors to reach two more sets of stairs and our next achievement opportunity. You need to jump from where you are to the area across the gap. I actually nailed it my first try when I originally got the achievement and thought "wow this is easy", but as you can see in the video walkthrough I had a tough time doing it again, and doing the cheese method is also tough because your body can go under the platform. So what you need to do is run with your arms in the air (waving them like you just don't care is optional) towards the gap and jump as close to the end of the platform as you possibly can. Once you latch on to the other area, pull yourself up and you'll unlock:

 

Mind the gap!

Take a big shortcut in the level "Mansion"

 
2 guides

 

Now once you're in your new area, you'll see a massive statue of yourself(?) standing over a fountain. Walk up the stairs to the left or right of this fountain and walk around to the fence behind the statue and stand so you're right behind its head. Climb up onto the fence and then from here, jump and climb on top of the statues head. Once you're standing on it, you'll unlock:

 

Pigeon Simulator

Stand on the head of the statue in the intro

 
2 guides

 

Now jump down and climb back up the stairs and continue on and you'll see a door with a green exit sign above it. To open this door, simply hold out your arms so they're pressing both buttons at the same time. Once you do this, the door will open. Walk through and you'll fall, unlocking:

 

Leap of Fail
