Act3, How to get the red key? | Walkthrough

 

 

  1. Now, you have to obtain the red key. Go towards the room in which you found the crowbar and the umbrella. Stop by the lever visible in the screenshot above. You cannot switch it in a regular way - throw a shoe at it.
  1. This will open the nearby compartment hiding the red key. Pick it up.
  1. Now, go to the track with the train car. It stops inside buildings - this wat, you can exit and steer it. Stop it as it is shown above. This is the room you have visited before (from here, you jumped into the room where you turned the train on). Apart from stopping the train, you need to put boxes and an armchair in front of it.
  1. Jump onto the pipe to the right, then on the car's roof. Next, jump onto the house roof visible in the screenshot.
  1. Now, you get to the roof with trees. Jump between trees until you reach the hatch visible above. Open it and throw e.g. a cardboard box inside.
  1. Next to the hatch there is the roof from which you can walk on the pipe to then jump to the building visible above. To do this, you have to have an umbrella - without it, you will not be able to reach the building. Inside, you can find the door with the red lock. Use the red key to go inside. This way, you get to a new location.
  1. Use the furniture to go up. Upon reaching the spot visible above, throw a cup at the shelf. This will allow you to continue towards the Exit sign.
  1. From there, jump to the toaster that will shoot you out to the higher shelves.
  1. Here, you can find darts (pick them up, they will prove handy later on) and the paper airplane. Push the plane and enter it to fly onto another shelf.
  1. Keep going up. Here, you need to use the darts (without them, you cannot reach the higher shelves). Darts pound in the furniture, so use them to climb higher.
  1. Finally, jump towards the lamp. During the "flight", you will see information prompting you to use the jump key again. Do it and you will reach the location. From now on, your character can perform a double jump.
