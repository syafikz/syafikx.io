REDSTONE HOTEL

 

CREATED BY ORELUED, SEBALP

Made by orelued, sebalp, (Flori_Gaming, RobiBlocks)

This is an art nouveau hotel with major red stone integration. A functional keycard system leads you to three types of rooms.

The first one being the basic, the second being the luxury apartment with views over the main entrance area and the most important being the ultra luxury apartment with stunning ceiling lights. Genuine art nouveau features and many other amenities that offer an unique experience.

Additionally you can find a dining area, a grand theatre room with an integrated restaurant and a large swimming area pared with an outside pool. The library and the bar on the upper floors guarantee a realistic experience of architecture and old world charm.

Don't forget to use the multi floor elevator and also check out the lighting system.
