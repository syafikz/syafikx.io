Tips 7

Trading tips. In trading, you can trade your items to other players for other items. You can't really go wrong in trading but before you click accept, think first if it's worth it or that you wouldn't regret it in the long run. Because they can easily leave the game and your pet will be gone forever. STAY AWAY FROM TRUST TRADES. Yes, a very small percentage of trust traders are quite honest but MOST are NOT. This is just a tactic to get your beloved pets. Don't Do This Please. A "fair" trade can mean nothing to be honest. As long as you really want something, and you don't care about the item you are trading then it's a done deal. I once got scolded by my brother for trading my penguin for a Pig. I don't regret it at all because all that's on my mind was "now I have Hawk from seven deadly sins".
