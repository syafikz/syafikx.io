The Pale City

Now, you’re back on the streets of the Pale City. First, head back down the alley and you’ll find a sewer grate pushed to the side. Take the ladder down and this will lead you to glitching remains. Then, climb back up and head up the alley, then go right. You’ll start to hear what sounds like bodies dropping. At first they’re in the distance, but they’ll start to drop closer. You’ll soon reach an open door on the right, so head inside.

This will lead you into an apartment building. Follow the stairs up until you hear music coming out of an open door on your right. Something will come crashing through the ceiling and crawl away. This is a Viewer.

Follow it to the right, and you’ll see it smashed its head into a TV. You’ll have to jump on that TV in order to reach the window, but don’t worry, the Viewer won’t come after you. From the ledge outside, jump down onto the awning and towards the one behind you. Take the ladder up, then jump to the next one and climb through that window.

In this next area you’ll run into several more Viewers. However, they’re obsessed with their TV’s, so as long as those are on you’re safe. Make your way down the hall and down the next hall a bit until you find a hole in the wall to crawl through.

 

In this next room, Six will boost you up to open the door on the far right. This will lead you into a room with a Viewer watching TV in the bathroom. Crawl through the vent in the wall on the opposite side and you’ll end up in a hallway.

Continue moving forward and you’ll find an elevator with a switch inside. Hop down towards it and flip it. This will take you up, but you’ll need a key to get through, so head back down.

While heading down, you’ll notice a hole in the wall on the right and some planks you can climb on. Hit the switch in the elevator again and, while it's going back up, jump over to this platform and climb up. Start making your way up and you’ll pop up right in front of the locked elevator.

Head to the room on the left and you’ll find the key in the first drawer on the bottom right. Once you’ve got it, unlock the elevator. Six will move over to boost you up to the switch on the left, but there are some things you’ll have to do first.

This puzzle is a little tricky, but mainly, the switch on this floor brings the elevator back up. So, head inside the elevator and flip the switch so it goes down, then run back out. Once it hits the bottom. Have Six boost you up again to pull the switch so it comes back up.

Now, wait a bit, then jump on the top of the elevator. This will lift you up to the next floor. From this area, move the box on the left to reveal a vent. Climb through here to find glitching remains. Now, head back to the main area and go through the hole in the wall on the right. This will lead you back outside.

You’ll be able to see the Signal Tower from here. Head right and climb through the window next to the door. Here, you’ll find more glitching remains. Then, pull the cage towards the door to open it and meet back up with Six.

Then, climb up the wall to the awning and make your way across the roof. You’ll find a contraption you can use to carry Six across. She’ll boost you up to reach it, then just turn it until the hanger is near her and turn it again to get her across. Once she’s on the other side, jump over and she’ll catch you.

Climb up both ladders until you reach a broken one. Then, climb the pipe next to it up to the open window. Crawl through and you’ll be back in a hallway. Unfortunately, this whole building is crumbling, so you’ll have to run through it. When you reach the door, push on it with Six’s help to get through.

Keep running until it all comes crumbling down. When the cutscene ends, rescue Six and make your way to the door on your right. She’ll boost you up to open it, then head down the hallway towards the open door on your right.

Here, you’ll find another TV to interact with. This cutscene will introduce you to the Thin Man. He’ll come through the TV screen to hunt down you and Six, so get ready to run. Run and hide in the room opposite of where you came in, underneath the bed.

The Thin Man will come in the room and capture Six. Now, you have to find her.

Once he leaves, head out of this room and back to the room with the TV. You’ll be sucked through the screen and dropped in a random room. Once you’re back on your feet. Climb up the bookshelf in front of you to the chair, then to the wooden floor boards above you.

You’ll find another TV here to interact with that will drop you outside. Now, from here, turn the crank to the left of the TV and move it all the way to the right. Then, head through the door on the left and back inside to use the portal again. This time, you’ll be dropped by the ladder.

Now, climb up and through the hole in the wall. Then, drop down onto the awning and continue heading right. When you reach the hook, jump to it and swing across. Then, keep going right until you reach a window.

Climb through, and you’ll end up in another room with a TV on the other side. Climb down the ladder and you’ll find a remote lying in front of the TV. Pick up the remote and point it at the TV to turn it on. Then, interact with the TV to transport to the one on the upper floor.

Head through the door here and you’ll enter into a post office where you'll find another TV. However, you can’t reach this one, so jump to the other side and continue forward until you enter into a larger room with a TV on the bottom. Drop down and turn it on with your remote.

If you drop down to use the vent instead of jumping to the other side at first, you can find a collectible hat in the vent opposite of the vent you go through to reach the larger room.

You’ll have to find a way to get across this area. There’s a dolly in the back of this room with some boxes on it that you can move to the center as a way to jump over. Once you’ve done that, use the TV to transport back to where you were.

Once you’re across, head up to the big hole in the wall and grab a hold of the hanger, which will take you into another building. However, before heading through the door on the right, head back out that window and back towards the open window behind this one. In here, you’ll find glitching remains next to a very familiar looking porcelain statue of the Lady. Then, hop back out and over to the window you went in before and through the door.

A Viewer will be camped in front of the TV, so sneak around them into the kitchen. From here, hop on the counter by the sink and turn on the TV outside. Then, head back to the living room and turn that one off so the Viewer goes to look at the one outside. Then, turn the living room one back on, hop through it, and you’ll wind up outside.

Cross the plank and climb the ladder. Continue going up until you reach another Viewer. Turn on the TV across from you, then turn off the one the Viewer’s in front of. They’ll end up falling off the building, so head through the one on your side to go to the other side.

Go through the hole in the wall on the right and down to the awnings. Once you reach the hanger, grab it to slide down. In this next area, you’ll see several Viewers jumping from the building while staring into the Signal Tower. 

They won’t notice you, so move past them until you reach a hole in the floor. This will drop you into a room with another TV. However, before turning it on, go through the window on your right and push over the other TV so you’ll land in another building rather than outside. Then, turn on the TV and head through.

In this next area, you’ll notice a lever behind you. Don’t worry about that right now. First, go up to the door on your right and pull off some of the planks to lure in the Viewer. Then, run over to the lever and pull it. This will send an electric shock through the water, killing the viewer.

Make sure to flip it back before heading for the door. Then, head to your right. You’ll find another TV to turn on here then climb through the vent on the right. You’ll land on a random pile of trash, so just jump on it to continue heading down.

Once you’re back on your feet, head into the room on the left. You’ll need to reach the switch by the elevator, so grab the box tied up in rope and move it underneath the switch to stand on. Then, flip the switch and turn on the TV in the elevator.

This TV will take you back up to the one you turned on previously, but for now, flip the switch in this elevator to head up. Once you get to the top, flip the switch again for the elevator to head down, then quickly interact with the TV.

From the top floor, quickly jump down onto the top of the elevator shaft and jump off at the floor between the bottom level and the one you were just on. Then, head right down the hallway. You’ll come across a box blocking a vent. So, just move it out of the way and climb through.

This next area is tricky, since you don’t want to get electrocuted by touching the water and there’s a Viewer in the next room. So, first, climb on top of the toilet and jump over to the open spot by the doorway.

You’ll need to grab the stool next to the Viewer in order to open the door on the right. However, if you get too close, the Viewer will attack you. So, head back into the bathroom doorway and shut off the TV. Then, jump back on the toilet. The Viewer will come charging toward you, but will be electrocuted once they step in the water. Now, you can grab the stool to open the door.

Now you’re back outside. You’ll see a group of Viewers crowded around a TV, but thankfully, you don’t have to go anywhere near them. Just hop in the window on the right.

In this next area, there’s a switch on the left wall that you’ll have to flip in order to navigate the water and not get electrocuted. So, head to the right and you’ll find a shopping cart that you can use. There's also a collectible hat on the back left shelf that you can reach with the shopping cart. After you’ve done that, push the cart in the water and hop on the shelves to your right, then jump on the shopping cart and follow the path over to the door on the left.

Head through the door and climb up the desk in the back left corner. Then, jump to the book shelf and climb through the vent. This will lead you right to the switch, which you can flip. After doing that, drop down and grab the shopping cart again.

Position the cart between the first shelf you jumped on and the TV on the right. Then, go back and flip the switch to turn the power back on. You can use the boxes next to the switch to safely head back to the top flooring. Now, you can make your way to the TV.

Once you pop out of the second TV, climb on top of it and head through the window. Drop down on the awning and you’ll see several more Viewers down the alleyway and a couple beneath you. Drop down to the ground, and you’ll notice a TV ahead of you, but it’s behind glass.

So, for this next area, you’ll have to turn off the TV to your left in order to attract the Viewers to the one on the right. Once they leave, turn on the TV to the left and interact with it. However, this will attract the entire group behind you, so be ready to run. The chase scene will have you zig-zagging through the store, but at the end you’ll find a TV to jump through. 

Once you’re on the other side, head right and into a room with another TV. This time around, Six will appear, so head up to the screen and grab onto her to try and pull her out. Unfortunately, the Thin Man will appear again and grab her. He’ll then pop out of the screen and come after you.

Run for the door and use the axe on the floor to break it open. Once you’re through, climb up the panels on the right and drop down into the next area. Crouch under the pipes and climb the panels on the right into the hole.

You’re under the floorboards now, so move quietly. The Thin Man will reappear above you, so avoid holes in the floor as he’s walking by. Once you’re on the other side, head through the hole and you’ll drop down into a hallway. 

Grab the stool nearby and push it under the window on the right. Be quick, though, because the Thin Man is coming towards you from down the hallway. Once you’re by the window, pull off the plank and run through. You’ll go sliding down the awning and down a train, but be ready to grab onto the platform or else you’ll fall.

Once you’re in the train, keep heading right. Unfortunately, the Thin Man will reappear and a chase scene will ensue. Keep running through the trains until you reach a lever. Then, pull it to separate your train car from where he is.

Once you’re back on your feet, you’ll notice Six’s glitching remains on the left. Keep following her. She’ll lead you up and into an alley that leads to stairs. Take them up and you’ll see a ladder, then take that up. This will lead you back to the streets.

The Thin Man will appear again, and now it’s time to fight him. He’ll approach you with his hand out, and Mono will do the same. You’ll have to handle this like how you would the TV's, by "tuning" the frequency to keep him back. Mono will use his powers to push the Thin Man back until he's defeated.
