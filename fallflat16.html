Human Fall Flat Level 16 - City

Level 16 - City

Text walkthrough:

After the slight break of Golf, this update level is back to the usual complexity of the past few levels, but again, it is not especially difficult to complete. As with every post-Ice level, this can be found in the Extra Dreams section.

As you drop into the level, you'll be right next to a yellow battery. Grab this and walk up the stairs to place it in its slot, then stand on the green circle on the platform and you'll shortly be launched over to another roof. We land right by our first Bowling Lane, and this one has to be completed to progress into the level. Grab one of the bowling balls to the left of it and carefully place it right in front of the lever behind the lane so it doesn't roll away, then jump up behind the lever. Pull the lever back and watch the lane ahead, and when you think the path will be clear for the ball, let go of the lever to push the ball. The best timing for this seems to be when all the obstacles are on the right and about to move back over to the left.

Once you've knocked down the pin at the end of the lane (Bowling Lane 1/4), it will open the way ahead as indicated by a green arrow. Follow the path onwards to enter a room with a giant fan and head out to the left. To the right here is a puzzle where we have to move the stacks into place. Push the green button to move the lines on the ground, then push the tallest stack into the middle. Press the button again and push the stack back over to the wall by the door we just went through. Head back and push the medium stack straight across to the tallest stack. Lastly, press the green button again and push the smallest stack into the middle, then press the button once more and move the smallest stack into place behind the others.

Now clamber up the stacks to the ledge at the top with another battery and yellow box. Push the box off the edge, then grab the battery and jump down with it to place it in its circular slot below. This lowers the wall on the right. Head back to grab the box and take it through to this new room. You'll see two ramps on the right; go up one of them and place the box into the yellow square, then grab the other box here and place it in the square on the other ramp. Now step on the two yellow buttons nearby to move both boxes into place on the other side of the room.

You'll see that they've formed a staircase, so climb up them to a ledge with another battery. Grab this and jump down to place it in the next slot. By the first box we just climbed will now be an open door; head through this and ride the fan that's been turned on all the way to a new area.

You'll come out by two red buttons and several pairs of sticks with red hands on them. Grab onto one of these pairs to get yourself a crossbow. This crossbow will fire by itself without you having to press anything, and if you stop grabbing at any point after this, it will disappear. With a crossbow in hand, step onto the rightmost button. You'll see a target come up at the bottom of the far building. Aim at this to land a shot on the target, adjusting your aim as you need to. Once you've hit it, the four targets above will light up. Go ahead and hit these too. After the first one, you have a slight timer to contend with as you'll see the green rim of the targets start to fade away, but it's a fairly generous amount of time to hit all the targets with.

Once you've done this and the button below you has turned green, move over to the next button. You'll have two targets to hit on the left and right, again with a slight timer, then two more will appear on the middle building. With those ones taken care of, turn around and you'll see two blue targets have appeared. Shoot both of these to lower some platforms. We're done with the crossbow now, so jump across the platforms to the next area.

Head straight through the middle of this area, to the far end where the roof ends. Turn left here, and behind a black pillar, you'll find a yellow Ragdoll 1/3. Throw this off the building. Now head back into the middle. You'll see a platform that you can move with a blue lever sticking out of it. Push this along the line on the ground until it is next to the other platform with a lever, then push the levers on both of them to raise the platforms as high as they can go.

Now head around behind the second platform and you'll see a grey ramp being held up by a blue stick. Move the stick to drop the ramp down, then head up it and climb your way up the two platforms. Jump across to another ramp. At the top of this one, you'll be next to several spinning blue platforms. Carefully jump across these to get around to the right of the fence in the middle, then to a platform with a yellow lever on the other side. Pull this and it will lower down a platform from above. Jump onto this and press the red button, then quickly jump off towards the next roof when it stops at the top, holding your arms out so you can climb up onto it.

You're in an area with several basketballs and ramps, and spinning hoops in the middle. Grab a basketball before heading up one of the ramps when the nearest hoop is nearly in line with it. If you get the timing right, you'll get sent flying straight into the backboard. Drop the ball into the hoop, or just drop into the hoop with it, and the spinning contraption will light up green. Head into the middle of it and you'll be sucked up into a light tunnel which eventually drops you into another new area.

Move around the right side of this roof to an air vent next to a blue platform. Climb up onto the vent and the platform, then turn left and swing across the bars to the third platform. Turn left again from here to see four blue ropes moving from the middle of the area to each of the corners. When the nearest blue rope stops in your corner, jump and grab onto it then let go when you're above the platform in the middle. Carefully grab either of the two ropes in front of you when it's safe and ride them over to the next platform. Stepping onto the yellow circle on the ramp here launches you onwards.

Climb up the two air vents on the right from where you land, then swing your way across the set of bars, using only one hand so you can grab onto the next bar with the other hand. Once you're across, grab onto the little blue stick next to the bowling balls here to move the bar in their way and let them drop down to the ground. We have another Bowling Lane to complete here, and this is also necessary to progress. Grab one of the balls and head into the space behind the lane, under the giant yellow hammer. If you look carefully at the ground here, you should see a lighter blue rectangular section; place the ball right in the middle of this, then step on the yellow button nearby. If you placed the ball correctly, the hammer should knock the ball right the way down the lane, hopefully knocking them all down in one or two goes.

Once they're all down (Bowling Lane 2/4), head to the far end of the area towards the now-active light tunnel. Head up all of the stairs here before jumping into the tunnel which sucks you all the way to your next area. When you reach the end, you'll have to move yourself to get off onto the roof.

We're now in the large final area where we'll actually start unlocking all the achievements. We'll begin by mopping up the Bowling Lanes and Ragdolls. (If you are playing this DLC when those achievements are still unobtainable and just want the two obtainable achievements, feel free to skip down to after the Temper Tantrum achievement.)

On the edge here, you'll see lots of drones lined up, and we'll be using these to get about. (These don't count as vehicles for the Hitchhiker achievement.) Grab one of these and wait on the building until the drone starts to lift up into the air, then fly off to the left and behind you. You're looking for a building with a neon sign of a basketball being dunked into a hoop; it's connected to the light tunnel by a green rope. Once you've located it, float down to land on top of the building.

Here, you'll find our next Bowling Lane. This one has a catapult to launch the balls with. Grab the yellow stick on the side of the catapult to lower it and place a ball into it, then carefully move it into place using the stick so that the yellow line coming from the back of the catapult goes straight through the middle orange pin behind it (the one that is lit). Now stand right behind the catapult and, taking as much care as you can not to move the catapult, push the blue lever on it up to send the ball flying. If the catapult moves slightly when you grab the lever, it is possible to move it back before pushing the lever up. This can take some real trial and error, but eventually you'll get all the pins knocked down (Bowling Lane 3/4).

On this roof, you'll find some more drones. Grab one of them and wait again until it starts to lift up, then fly directly 90 degrees to the right. You're looking for a stack of blue rings which indicates a fan that will blow you higher up in the air. Move into this to get lifted up as high as possible, then fly straight past the orange coil you can see below. To the left of another stack of rings, you'll see a building down below with more drones and another Bowling Lane on it. Float down until you can land on top of this building.

This Lane requires you to fire the balls with a cannon. Grab one of the balls nearby and place it into the cannon which will cause it to light, then get around behind it and grab the lever to align the yellow line coming from it straight through the middle line of the yellow dots further behind it. It will fire after a few seconds and if your aim was true, it will hopefully knock down all the pins in one go (Bowling Lane 4/4). At this point, when the achievement is fixed, you should unlock:

 

Tip-Top Ten Pin

Complete all 4 bowling lanes

Now let's move onto the remaining two Ragdolls. Jump off the side of this building to respawn back in the central building. Straight ahead from the edge where the drones are, you'll see another stack of rings, and if you look just right from that, there'll be a tower with an antenna for it. The next Ragdoll is on top of this antenna, and if you look carefully, you might be able to faintly see the green Ragdoll from here.

When you're ready, grab a drone (preferably with just one hand so we can try and grab the Ragdoll in a moment) and when it lifts you up, fly over to the rings straight ahead. Once the fan has taken you up as high as possible, carefully float down to the top of that antenna. If you didn't see it before, you'll definitely see the Ragdoll come into view as you get closer. Try and pick it up in your free hand if you can so you can just let go of the drone and drop down to the streets with it in hand to get it out of the level. If you end up knocking it off instead, you might have to fly around the tower to look for it because it might not fall all the way to the ground; grab it to finish the job if this happens (Ragdoll 2/3).

Once you've dealt with it, respawn back at the central building if you haven't already and grab another drone, then fly 90 degrees to the left. You want to fly straight over the building with the basketball hoops below (with the light tunnel in the middle of it), looking down as you do so to find a building with a giant blue tunnel in its side. Float down into this tunnel and when it's safe, drop down to the floor. Right in the middle of this is our final Ragdoll, this one being red. Grab this and throw it out of the tunnel (Ragdoll 3/3). At this point, when the achievement is fixed, you should unlock:

 

Temper Tantrum

Knock all 3 rag dolls off the level

 
2 guides

 

Let's knock out the last miscellaneous achievement before we finish the level (well, only our first achievement if the previous two are still unobtainable). Respawn back at the central building and grab another drone, then look down to the streets below you as you start floating downwards. You're looking for either a green or red car that is circling the streets around the buildings. When you've located one of them, float down towards it so you can try and land on it. You can grab onto it with one of your arms and hold on for 10 seconds, or what also seemed to work for me was standing on top of the car whilst still holding onto the drone and moving with it for 10 seconds. It can be a bit of a nuisance to get onto the car, but once you've successfully ridden it for 10 seconds, you'll unlock:

 

Hitchhiker

Ride one of the vehicles for 10 seconds

 
2 guides

 

Now we can go ahead and finish the level properly. If you get back to the central building and look at the exit building directly across from the drones, you'll see four colours above it. We need to fly around the area and break all of the coils that match these colours to open the exit hole and leave here.

If you look slightly left from that building, underneath the light tunnel, you should be able to spot the first green coil. Grab a drone to fly down to this coil and crash into it to break it. Don't let go of the drone just yet though. You might have already seen the blue coil on your way to this one, but if not, fly from the back-left corner of this building (the opposite corner from the green coil) to find another building below with the blue coil. Float down and break this one too, then just drop off the edge to respawn again.

Grab another drone, but before flying off the building, head to the right to find another building with the orange coil (you flew past it earlier if you did the Bowling Lanes). Fly down to crash into that coil. From this building, to your left, you should quite clearly be able to see the building with the pink coil on it because of the giant pink wire going up it. Fly straight over to the rings you can see by that building and let this fan lift you up before floating back down to break the final coil. With all the coils broken, the exit hole will have opened, so float down from here to land in it and finish. You'll unlock:

 

Sports Fan

Complete "City" level

Well, until these two unobtainable achievements are fixed, if you've completed everything else, you should have 77/79 achievements and 1880/1960 Gamerscore. Hopefully these achievements are fixed soon. Also be aware that there are supposedly more levels that are in the works that have yet to be released, so in case you haven't learned already, don't assume that your time with this game is done just yet!
